# 7 Awesome Street Style Looks from Lauren Conrad ...

There are tons of amazing street style looks from Lauren Conrad, the fashion guru of so many. She always looks so put-together and is always rocking the latest trends. Luckily for us all, she helps us stay in the loop by blogging about the latest fashions. She's come so far in the fashion world and looks just as fabulous walking down the street as she does at any red carpet event. Check out some of my favorite street style looks from Lauren Conrad!

## 1. Spring Chic

![Spring Chic](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o7/mp/cfuu5mmr_550x825.jpg)

Lauren looks effortlessly chic no matter what. Am I right, ladies? For spring, she's all about loose-fitting blouses tucked into shorts, and of course, some wedges to elongate the legs! This is a look that can easily be recreated with clothes from Forever 21. It's cheap and easy, and you'll be earning major style points like LC! This is just one of the awesome street style looks from Lauren Conrad.

## 2. Print + Jeans

![Print + Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tp/bh/0q4pvkjp_468x750.jpg)

Whenever you don't know what to wear, grab some skinnies and a shirt with a fun print on it. You can never go wrong with that. Lauren keeps her street style really casual whenever she's just running errands, etc. Black skinnies and a cheetah-print top, paired with oversized sunglasses and a high bun, will make the perfect go-to outfit.

## 3. Denim on Denim

![Denim on Denim](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qr/j2/rdo9nobv_350x583.jpg)

This spring, try the whole denim on denim look! Lauren rocks this look from time to time and looks fab. You can rock dark-wash skinnies with a light chambray shirt, or just rock a denim jacket over one of your favorite tees. It might sound like too much, but denim on denim always looks great. For shoes, go with something casual like converse or combat boots!

## 4. Business Chic

![Business Chic](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ll/x6/dwb01gbq_600x857.jpg)

I love that Lauren always looks super classy and business-ready. It's actually such an easy look to achieve. For a business casual look, rock some dark skinnies with a tucked-in button up shirt. Throw a blazer over it and bam! All done. For shoes, I love nude-colored flats. I think they give a touch of sophistication.

## 5. Midi Chic

![Midi Chic](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d7/m5/otr6xa56_433x650.jpg)

I've been seeing midi skirts everywhere and I am so in love with this trend. Lauren rocks it with a really simple outfit that's super easy to recreate. Personally, I am a huge fan of navy blue paired with white. So, rocking a white tank tucked into a navy blue midi skirt sounds amazing! You can find some adorable midi skirts at [ASOS](https://goo.gl/kAax2z).

## 6. Floral

![Floral](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ay/h0/h30mfefn_395x594.jpg)

You can never go wrong with floral. It's the perfect look to recreate for spring! You can find tons of adorable and affordable floral dresses on [Forever 21](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com). I love dresses with huge flowers - it's a fun way to make a fashion statement. How do you feel about the floral trend?

## 7. Pop of Color

![Pop of Color](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y8/ki/qok95x8i_553x657.jpg)

I love keeping an outfit really neutral and then adding a pop of color. An easy way to do this to rock a black tee with some vibrant colored high-waisted shorts. You can opt for a bright orange or even swap them for more pastel-colored ones that are perfect for spring!

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of many awesome street style looks from Lauren Conrad. She's become quite the fashionista and I love keeping up with her latest looks. If you follow her Instagram (@laurenconrad), you'll see more fashion-related pictures. Of the list, which look is your favorite? Which one will you be trying to recreate?

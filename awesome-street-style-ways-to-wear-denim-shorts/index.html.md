# 9 Awesome Street Style Ways to Wear Denim Shorts ...

Denim shorts are a wardrobe staple and there are plenty of different ways to wear denim shorts this season. Stick with a laid-back look in cool cut-offs or work a more polished angle in tailored denim. Don’t just limit your denim shorts to beachwear, either. Take a look at these street style-inspired ways to wear denim shorts.

## 1. Tailored

![Tailored](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/07/1_tailored_432x648.jpg)

When it comes to ways to wear denim shorts, most people tend to stick with casual, laid-back looks. However, Kelly Stuart shot Elena for Elle Magazine working a much more polished look. Elena worked a relaxed tailored look in light denim shorts, a vintage collared shirt, and white Silence & Noise blazer. Lace up ankle boots and layered necklaces completed the look.

## 2. Sporty and Laid-back

![Sporty and Laid-back](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/07/2_sporty-and-laidback_535x829.jpg)

The combination of denim shorts, a t-shirt, and sneakers is a signature summer look. Claire was snapped by Sophie Mabille rocking a cool green t-shirt tucked into dark denim Levi’s shorts. Extra points for incorporating a skateboard into a street style shot.

## 3. Casually Eclectic

![Casually Eclectic](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/07/3_casually-eclectic_426x639.jpg)

Aliaa Elesseily was snapped by Dvora for Vogue Magazine in this eclectic get-up. She teamed her ripped Topshop denim shorts with a Kenzo printed sweater, embroidered tote bag, and studded loafers. The outfit is full of bold statement pieces, yet they all work to create a casually eclectic look.

## 4. Classic Pairings

![Classic Pairings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/07/4_classic-pairings_592x888.jpg)

Angela was snapped by Candice Lake in this classic pairing. She teamed a classic white shirt with distressed denim shorts and flat ankle boots. A chic fedora and studded Alexander Wang bag completed the street-smart look. The pairing of a white shirt and denim shorts is quite timeless and can be dressed up or down with trend-based accessories.

## 5. Coated

![Coated](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/07/5_coated_592x888.jpg)

When the weather gets a bit chilly, just throw on an oversized coat. Rose Gray was photographed for Glamour Magazine wearing this stylishly simple outfit. She teamed her H&M denim shorts with a semi-sheer white top, Zara Jacket, and tan Topshop sandals. The rounded metal sunglasses added a retro touch to the outfit.

## 6. Pastel and Prints

![Pastel and Prints](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/07/6_pastel-and-prints_600x900.jpg)

Blogger Nicolette Mason works a girly approach to denim by teaming her cuffed denim shorts with a pastel swan print blouse from ASOS and gold glitter sunglasses. Nude platform pumps and a sleek [black satchel](https://bags.allwomenstalk.com/best-black-satchel-bags/) complete this polished daytime look.

## 7. Camouflaged

![Camouflaged](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/07/7_camouflaged_380x570.jpg)

Blogger Willabelle from Pale Division mixes and matches trends with this outfit. She wears her denim cut-offs with an animal print t-shirt and camouflage jacket. A studded choker adds an edgy element to the outfit while the combination of socks and loafers is more of a preppy touch.

## 8. Double Denim

![Double Denim](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/07/8_double-denim_600x900.jpg)

Take a leaf out of Laura from On The Racks’ book and try your hand at double denim this season. Try to create a matching set by choosing separates in the same kind of denim. Here, Laura opted for relaxed runner-style chambray denim shorts and a matching collared shirt. A cream top breaks up the denim on denim look.

## 9. Off Duty

![Off Duty](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/07/9_off-duty_600x893.jpg)

Recreate a model-off-duty look by teaming your denim shorts with a leather biker jacket. This lady was spotted on the Stylesight blog wearing a quilted leather jacket, grey marle top, cuffed denim shorts, and black ballet flats. Aviator sunglasses, a cream tote, and chunky watch finished off this street-smart look.

These are just a few street-style inspired ways to wear your denim shorts. Which of these outfits do you like best? How will you be styling your denim shorts this season?

# 7 Boho Street Style Outfits from Vanessa Hudgens You'll Love ...

There are so many awesome street style outfits from Vanessa Hudgens. If it's not her official title, I nominate Vanessa to be called "Boho Queen." Her street style is effortless, chic, and classic all at the same time. Honestly, I would love the chance to raid her closet. I think I'd have to borrow just about everything! Check out some of my favorite boho street style outfits from Vanessa Hudgens.

## 1. Lace + Maxi

![Lace + Maxi](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y5/vb/qyu38mla_600x900.jpg)

Lace is one of my favorite things to wear because it's sexy and sophisticated. Since it's such a statement pattern, I'd pair it with solid colors like a classic black maxi. Black and white ALWAYS look good together because it's timeless! You'll definitely look and feel like a boho queen in this ensemble. This is just one of many street style outfits from Vanessa Hudgens.

## 2. Crop Top + Denim

![Crop Top + Denim](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lp/a7/o15l5us3_600x839.jpg)

Boho looks can be really simple and still be as chic. Add a cute belt to your denim cutoffs and pair it with a printed crop top. The boho vibe is all about accessories - necklaces, bracelets, rings, you name it. So, don't be afraid to layer all of those things on. For shoes, I'd go with some combat boots but you can swap them for classic converse or some ballet flats.

## 3. Floral Maxi

![Floral Maxi](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eu/sm/e235vtr9_500x675.jpg)

You can never go wrong with a floral maxi. You can find some on [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/SVvcjh) I would pair it with a bold-colored top to add some contrast and make the outfit really pop. To complete the whole boho look, don't forget to add a floppy hat. That's Vanessa's signature look.

## 4. Festival-ready

![Festival-ready](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ur/of/brrhbzuc_500x674.jpg)

Unfortunately, the summer music festivals have come to an end this year. But, that doesn't mean you can't start planning your outfits for next year. Vanessa always keeps things simple and chic - denim cutoffs and knitted crop tops are always a good idea. You can accessorize with beaded necklaces, bracelets, hair feathers, etc. Which music festival would you wear this outfit to?

## 5. Kimono Cardigan

_ A kimono cardigan is the perfect addition to any outfit. You can pair it with high-waisted shorts and crop tops to keep you from looking and feeling 'too' exposed. For your kimono cardigan needs, try forever21.com_. Everything's cute AND affordable.

## 6. Flowy Dress

![Flowy Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xl/g1/cjn6kagh_460x745.jpg)

Every boho wardrobe needs a flowy dress! I'm pretty much obsessed with floral patterns so that's what I'd go for. I'd keep the accessories to a minimum because the pattern on the dress is already a statement piece in your outfit! If you want to wear jewelry, maybe stick to a long necklace and a couple of rings. For your hair, braids are very boho-chic!

## 7. Flared Pants

![Flared Pants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sk/gm/j5xxtrjs_360x534.jpg)

I'm not entirely sure I'd be able to pull these pants off but I LOVE the look of them. They're perfect for the transition from summer to fall because they're light and breezy! You can find cool patterned flared pants on [pacsun.com](http://www.pacsun.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com). I would pair the pants with a crop top for a finished look! Would you wear flared pants?

Well, there you have it. These are just seven out of many boho street style looks from Vanessa Hudgens. I think it's safe to say that no one does boho-chic like V does! Of the list, which look is your favorite? Which look(s) will you be recreating?

# 9 Chic Black and White Street Style Looks ...

Get your dose of this season’s monochrome trend by taking a look at some black and white street style looks. No longer confined to corporate offices, black and white ensembles are chicer than ever before. We’ve spotted a number of fashionable street style takes on this season’s must have monochrome trend. Take it back to basics with a little bit of inspiration from these black and white street style looks.

## 1. Black and White Basics

![Black and White Basics](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/29214852_9609_592x888.jpg)

Malin was snapped by Suzanne Middlemass in this casual black and white street style look. She wears a short black Topshop skater skirt with a basic white t-shirt and white Converse trainers. Wayfarer-style sunglasses and a simple white tote complete this effortlessly stylish black and white look.

## 2. Statement Black and White Skirt

![Statement Black and White Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/29214853_8540_600x900.jpg)

Aimee was photographed by Anton Lombardi for ELLE wearing a striking splatter print skirt. A slouchy white tank top with a black bra peeking out underneath add a casual touch to a quite formal skirt. Simple strappy heels, a bright clutch, and assorted jewellery complete this refreshing daytime look.

## 3. Colour Blocked Black and White

![Colour Blocked Black and White](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/29214853_4770_592x888.jpg)

Kaitlin was snapped by Glamour Magazine wearing a colour blocked black and white dress. She teams her Kooples dress with a structured Kate Spade bag and edgy black ankle boots. She also accessorised with oversized sunglasses, a chunky watch, and slim bangles.

## 4. Black and White and Leather

![Black and White and Leather](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/29214853_0571_600x937.jpg)

We spotted this black and white outfit on the Stylesight blog. The tailored black leather-look shorts teamed with a long sleeve white top make for a casually dressy look. The patent ankle boots and leather jacket tied around the waist also add some edge to the outfit. You could dress an outfit like this up or down depending on your shoes and accessories.

## 5. Casual Black and White Separates

![Casual Black and White Separates](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/29214852_2550_592x888.jpg)

Tabitha was snapped for Glamour Magazine wearing a basic black t-shirt with a short white pleated skirt. She works an alternative angle by teaming her outfit with vintage Dr Martens shoes and light blue ankle socks. The pleats give this outfit a preppy feel but the choice of footwear gives this outfit a bit more street cred.

## 6. Black and White with Pops of Colour

![Black and White with Pops of Colour](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/29214853_4357_600x900.jpg)

Helena was photographed for ELLE wearing a chic white blouse with leather-look pants. The basic white blouse balances out the trend-based design of the pants. Peep toe heels add a ladylike feel and the pops of colour from her handbag and lipstick add a fun touch to the overall look. The hat just tops it all off nicely.

## 7. Modern Elegance in Black and White

![Modern Elegance in Black and White](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/29214854_0916_600x901.jpg)

Maria was spotted by Stylesight wearing this elegant black and white outfit. The risqué cut out pencil skirt was balanced out with a prim and proper collared shirt. A compact clutch and patent ankle boots add modern touches to this elegant outfit.

## 8. Black and White Jeans and a Tee

![Black and White Jeans and a Tee](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/29214853_7217_592x888.jpg)

You can’t get more timeless than the combination of jeans and a t-shirt. Here, Pippa was photographed by Suzanne Middlemass wearing some acid wash black Cheap Monday jeans with a boxy white t-shirt. The brogue shoes add a masculine inspired touch to the outfit but this is immediately balanced out by the structured, ladylike handbag.

## 9. Black and White Layers

![Black and White Layers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/29214853_9452_432x648.jpg)

This layered black and white outfit was photographed by Alyssa Greenberg for ELLE. A casual layered look of black jeans, white shirt, and a leather jacket was given some extra edge with a black and white plaid shirt tied around the waist. It’s a casual look that’s perfect for hitting the city streets in.

Black and white outfits never go out of style and right now it’s all about these sleek monochrome looks. These are just some chic black and white street style looks we’ve spotted lately. Are you a fan of black and white outfits? Which of these street style looks do you like best?

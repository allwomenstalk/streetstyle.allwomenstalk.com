# 9 Colourful Winter Street Style Looks to Brighten up Your Day ...

Brighten up a dreary winter’s day by embracing some colourful winter street style looks. The idea that certain colours can enhance your mood is one that you could easily apply it to your seasonal wardrobe. Ditch the dull black and grey outfits and embrace all things colourful to help you get out of a seasonal rut. Be inspired to brighten up your wardrobe with these colourful winter street style looks.

## 1. Colour Clashing

![Colour Clashing](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/07202445_1298_600x900.jpg)

Colourful winter street style looks, like this outfit from blogger Pauline, are bound to brighten up a dull and dreary day. She layers a lemon yellow coat over a hot pink sweater and breaks up the look with some printed pants. A yellow contrasting handbag, orange heels, and a bright pink lip complete this colourful look.

## 2. Colour Blocking

![Colour Blocking](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/07202443_0478_432x648.jpg)

Ella was photographed by Amar Daved wearing this colour blocked outfit. A bold electric blue sweater was toned down with a light blue tailored jacket and peach coloured skirt. Strappy white heels anchor this cheery winter outfit.

## 3. Statement Coats

![Statement Coats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/07202443_0228_380x570.jpg)

An easy way to brighten up your winter outfit is with a colourful coat. There’s been a big trend for colourful coats lately and Mariann from Cashmere in Style works a polished everyday look in her lime green coat. She wears it over a basic outfit of a striped sweater and boyfriend jeans. A jewel necklace and silver pumps add some glamorous touches to the outfit.

## 4. Quirky Colours

![Quirky Colours](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/07202444_0923_600x857.jpg)

Aino was spotted on Hel Looks wearing this quirky colourful outfit. She accessorises a buttoned-up yellow coat with a hot pink scarf and lime green waist belt. A printed bag, ankle boots, and a slouchy cap complete this snow day look.

## 5. Practical Puffers

![Practical Puffers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/07202444_0148_600x888.jpg)

When the temperature drops, sometimes there’s no other option than to pull on a puffer jacket. While they’re not always the most stylish of items, this monochrome outfit photographed by Daniel Zuchnik challenges our perception of the puffer jacket. Here, an orange puffer jacket looks quite stylish teamed with a matching printed skirt and beanie.

## 6. Jewel Tones

![Jewel Tones](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/07202444_3770_600x884.jpg)

If bold, bright colours aren’t your style then you can always try jewel tones instead. Emerald and turquoise colours are always popular and can make for quite a bright outfit. We spotted this outfit on the Stylesight blog and while the jewel-toned sweater and pencil skirt combination is quite ladylike, the lace knee-high socks and camouflage print sneakers are quite unexpected.

## 7. Radiant Reds

![Radiant Reds](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/07202453_7889_600x900.jpg)

The red coat is a fashion favourite. It’s bold and will instantly add some glamour to your outfit. This bright outfit was photographed by Adam Katz Sinding. A tailored red coat looks perfectly polished when teamed with a matching red striped top. The faded denim jeans and ankle boots, however, add an off-duty touch to the outfit.

## 8. Colourful Pants

![Colourful Pants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/07202454_9096_532x800.jpg)

We spotted this colourful outfit on the Coggles street style blog. Brightly coloured pants are great for making a statement this winter. The combination of green pants and a coral top is quite bold but is toned down with the addition of a black leather jacket, ankle boots, and hat.

## 9. Pretty Pastels

![Pretty Pastels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/07202454_5629_600x902.jpg)

The pastel trend is still going strong for winter. This outfit featured on Vogue Mexico is just one example of how to colour block your pastels for winter. The combination of a coral coloured coat, pastel pink jeans, chunky lilac scarf, and black ankle boots is one you could try recreating for a cheery everyday look.

There are plenty of ways to incorporate colour into your outfits in winter. If head to toe colour isn’t your style, then maybe try one statement piece or a few colourful accessories instead. How do you like to wear colour in winter?

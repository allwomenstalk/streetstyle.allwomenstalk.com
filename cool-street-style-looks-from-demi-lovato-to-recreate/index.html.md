# 7 Cool Street Style Looks from Demi Lovato to Recreate ...

There are so many fantastic street style looks from Demi Lovato that are perfect to recreate! The girl is a serious fashionista. I love how she's never afraid to take any fashion risks. She's always switching up her style (and hair color) but I love every look she's had. Since she is constantly switching things up, these seven looks from Demi Lovato are some of my favorite from the past few years. Let me know which looks are your favorites!

## 1. Hipster Chic

![Hipster Chic](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vi/op/0i6vv30f_500x638.jpg)

I love this look! It screams hipster chic. Demi's always rocking round sunglasses, and I'm obsessed with them. You can find similar ones at [shop.nordstrom.com](https://www.nordstrom.com/browse/women/accessories/sunglasses/round-sunglasses). I'm also loving the oversized sweater and hat! She always looks super casual yet extremely stylish. This is just one of the awesome looks from Demi Lovato!

## 2. All Black

![All Black](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p4/ky/qaqpfed9_548x780.jpg)

Nobody pulls off an all-black ensemble like Demi does! I love everything about this outfit - the lace, boots, leather, graphic tee, oversized shades - need I go on? I think you can never go wrong with an all-black outfit. This is such a cute outfit for running errands, or going off on a shopping spree with your friends. Would you rock this look?

## 3. Rocker Chic

_ Demi's definitely into the 'rocker' look. I like that she still keeps things casual. A military jacket is a must-have in every girl's closet! You can find similar ones at forever21.com_. Rock your favorite graphic tee and pair it with some cool leather leggings. Don't forget the round shades and combat boots!

## 4. Floral

![Floral](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qz/rf/rnm57bf6_426x640.jpg)

I love it when Demi wears soft girly colors! She looks great in this matching floral set. To recreate this, you can rock it with a plain white tee or even a graphic tee! Keep the accessories to a minimum since the floral pattern is already a statement piece! You can never go wrong with floral, especially in the spring!

## 5. Girly Sophisticated

![Girly Sophisticated](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rf/ay/nprdqqz5_585x800.jpg)

I love this look. It's super casual, but very girly and sophisticated. It's the perfect ensemble to wear to the mall or to just grab lunch with your girl friends. I'm also obsessed with her side fish-tail braid. When she was rocking this look, it really made me want turquoise tips! I don't think I'd pull it off as amazingly as she does though! To recreate this look, just rock a white high-waisted skirt and pair it with a fun patterned shirt! I love that the shoes match the belt - it's a cool touch!

## 6. Metallic

![Metallic](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/va/gv/p0xt68zs_600x870.jpg)

Demi might be the only one who makes a metallic blazer look awesome. She rocked this look when she performed on the Jimmy Kimmel stage! Again, she's rocking leather! This is actually a really easy outfit to recreate - you can find metallic blazers at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)! To add some edge to your outfit, wear your fave graphic tee underneath the blazer.

## 7. Maxi Skirt

![Maxi Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zg/ua/twizkx99_600x904.jpg)

This is a really gorgeous outfit to recreate this spring! Pair your black maxi skirt (you can find these at Forever 21) with a sheer button up. It's cute and girly, but still has a cool "edge" to it!

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of many cool looks from Demi Lovato! She's definitely becoming quite the fashion icon. Of the list, which is your favorite look? Which outfits will you be recreating this spring/summer? Share outfit ideas and tips in the comments section!

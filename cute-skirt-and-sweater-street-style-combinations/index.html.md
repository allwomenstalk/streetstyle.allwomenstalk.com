# 9 Cute Skirt and Sweater Street Style Combinations ...

The skirt and sweater combination is a popular outfit option for fall and winter. From grunge-inspired looks to polished outfits, there are many variations on the combination. You can easily make this combo work to suit your own personal style. We’ve spotted plenty of cute skirt and sweater combos on the streets and street style snaps are a great way to find inspiration for your own outfits. Take a look at these following cute skirt and sweater combinations.

## 1. Casual Cable Knit

![Casual Cable Knit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/22220522_9366_486x730.jpg)

Ella was photographed by Wayne Tippetts wearing this cute skirt and sweater combo. The sea green cable knit sweater works well with the light denim skirt. The collar peeking out at the top and pom pom beanie add cute touches to the outfit. Tan boots and a bag complete this casual everyday look.

## 2. Minimalist Monochrome

![Minimalist Monochrome](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/22220522_0390_592x888.jpg)

Monochrome looks are really having a moment. Ivelina was spotted by Glamour wearing an [all white outfit](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/tips-on-how-to-wear-an-all-white-outfit/) teamed with black accessories. She teamed her white ASOS skirt with a Zara sweater and a beanie from H&M. The shiny boots and perforated bag add sleek and edgy touches to this otherwise ladylike look.

## 3. Sheer and Light

![Sheer and Light](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/22220522_5017_500x749.jpg)

Sheer skirts are a popular choice during the summer months, but you could always make them work in the cooler months with some artful layering. We spotted this cute skirt and sweater combo over on the Topshop Tumblr. The pastel purple sweater and sheer skirt create quite a girlie combination but the thigh-high lace-up boots add a racy touch to the entire look.

## 4. Tulle Tutu

![Tulle Tutu](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/22220522_0305_400x599.jpg)

Nothing says sweet and girlie dressing quite like a tulle tutu. Mark Iantosca photographed this tutu-clad lady for Refinery 29. The combination of the skirt and sweater is quite sweet but the hot pink sneakers, shiny metallic clutch, and chunky necklace add fun and unexpected elements to the outfit.

## 5. Fluffy and Flippy

![Fluffy and Flippy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/22220522_9873_500x750.jpg)

Wear your favourite summer skirt in between seasons by pairing it with a cosy sweater. This skirt and sweater combo was spotted by Topshop’s street style photographers. The fluffy sweater works well teamed with the flippy printed skirt with sandals. You could also switch the sandals for ankle boots for a more fall appropriate look.

## 6. Cartoon Print

![Cartoon Print](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/22220522_8244_500x750.jpg)

Quirky printed sweaters are a fun way to update your outfits this season. We spotted this downright cute look on the Topshop Tumblr. The Goofy cartoon print sweater looks like it could be a childhood favourite and it’s quite a quirky pairing when teamed with a high-fashion ruffled skirt.

## 7. Oversized Gingham

![Oversized Gingham](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/22220523_8941_600x899.jpg)

Gingham is certainly having a moment right now. Here, Liz was snapped by Mark Iantosca for Refinery 29 wearing a black and white gingham midi skirt teamed with a slouchy blue sweater. Patent platform heels, chunky jewellery, and statement sunglasses add contemporary touches to what would usually be a quite ladylike outfit.

## 8. Colour Clash

![Colour Clash](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/22220523_9441_599x898.jpg)

Blogger Blair Eadie of Atlantic-Pacific works a colour clashing combo. She teams a hot pink J.Crew Sweater with a bright red Tibi skirt. Red heels streamline this boldly modern take on ladylike dressing.

## 9. Layered

![Layered](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/22220524_5692_600x900.jpg)

Iris, from the blog A Dash of Fash, toughens up a preppy peplum skirt and sweater combo with the addition of a quilted bomber jacket and ankle boots. The blue and green colour combo is quite striking and is a great way of incorporating colour into your winter looks.

These are just some cute skirt and sweater combos, as seen on the streets. Which of these looks do you like best? How would you style a skirt and sweater combo this season?

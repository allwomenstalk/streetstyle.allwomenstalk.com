# 7 Fab Street Style Looks from Jessica Alba ...

There are so many fabulous street style looks from Jessica Alba that are fun and easy to recreate! Jessica's become quite the fashionista and looks amazing on and off the red carpet. I love that she keeps her street style very casual but still totally fashionable. She's definitely become one of my fashion gurus and this list will help you to see why. Check out some of my favorite street style looks from Jessica Alba!

## 1. Floral Maxi

![Floral Maxi](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fa/z4/abj6w25n_449x750.jpg)

You can never go wrong with floral, right? Especially in the spring time. Jessica's always rocking the floral trend but she always makes it her own by adding some fun little touches. For example, adding a denim vest and scarf can take your floral maxi to the next level! This is just one of the fab street style looks from Jessica Alba!

## 2. Flared Pants

![Flared Pants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jf/0o/uzl4f23j_350x550.jpg)

Jessica's definitely a fan of flared jeans! If anyone's bringing the look back, it's her. She even rocks the denim on denim look sometimes and seriously looks fab doing it. If you want to recreate the look, pick up some flared pants and team it with a chambray shirt. Or, you can always swap it for a fun graphic tee. Would you copy this look?

## 3. Pretty in Pink

![Pretty in Pink](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hv/iu/cir5pch5_500x807.jpg)

I like that Jessica never goes overboard on just one color. She always add a pop of variation. For a sleek and somewhat mod-style, pick a black long-sleeved crop top and pair it with a full-length baby pink skirt. It pairs together so nicely! You can team this look with either flats or strapped heels. Don't forget the high bun and oversized shades!

## 4. Neutrals

![Neutrals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z2/h4/uh2fi7n9_373x550.jpg)

Jessica is the master of neutral colors. I think it's a very sleek and sophisticated look for the spring. Instead of an all-black or all-white ensemble, opt for something neutral like a beige. An all-beige ensemble looks really sleek and you can add a pop of color to it to get you into spring fashion mode.

## 5. Rocker/girly

![Rocker/girly](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gb/ee/acy0wvi2_500x750.jpg)

My favorite type of style is when rocker meets girly. Jessica pulls off this look effortlessly! You can team up a super girly high-waisted skirt, some killer heels, and a pastel top. Then, to add some "edge" to the outfit, throw on your leather jacket. It'll instantly change up your outfit and you'll be looking as sweet and stylish as you ever did.

## 6. Printed Pants

![Printed Pants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wz/m9/i0s12dwh_445x720.jpg)

I love that Jessica isn't afraid to shout it out with bold prints. I think patterned pants paired with black boots and a black top looks great together! For a pop of color, you can add a handbag in a very vibrant color. You can find cool patterned pants at Forever 21! Is this a look for you?

## 7. Pastel Blazers

![Pastel Blazers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k0/ml/nsx6upwm_396x594.jpg)

Jessica's definitely a fan of keeping things sweet and simple. She's always adding pastel touches to her outfit which is perfect for the spring. One of the fun ways to wear pastels is in a blazer. It would look great on top of some dark skinnies and white tank top. You can find some pastel blazers at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/2DJUC2). It's a cute feminine touch to any outfit!

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of many fabulous street style looks from Jessica Alba. This woman looks great no matter what. Of the list, which look is your favorite? Which look will you be trying to recreate this spring?

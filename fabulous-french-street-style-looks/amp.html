All Black • A Flawless Frock • Layered up • Denim Abroad • Stripe-tastic • More ...

By Lyndsie

I've always been obsessed with French street style, and my upcoming Paris honeymoon has me even more obsessed. French women dress with a confidence, an eye, an attention to detail, and a certain insouciance that makes them stand out. Because the most fashionable women know the value of classic pieces that fit beautifully, the classic French street style is easy to imitate even if you don't have a designer or couture budget. Most of all, to me, French women prove that the size of your pants and your age don't matter. As long as you own what you wear and choose timeless pieces, you'll look amazing. Frequently Asked Questions

How can I achieve that effortless French street style look?
Oh, it's all about mastering simplicity with a dash of chic. Think neutral colors, well-fitted clothes, and timeless pieces. A nice pair of jeans, a classic white shirt, and some stylish flats or loafers are key!
What are some must-have items for Parisian street fashion?
You've got to have a good trench coat, a striped Breton shirt, and of course, some well-fitted jeans. And don't forget a stylish scarf and a cute beret if you're feeling extra French!
How do French women make casual style look so elegant?
French women have this knack for mixing high and low fashion seamlessly. They opt for high-quality basics and elevate their look with a stylish accessory or two. It's all in the details, really.
Is there a difference between French and Parisian street style?
While they are very similar, Parisian street style tends to be a bit more sophisticated and polished. Think effortless chic with a touch of urban elegance. French street style overall is a bit more diverse and can vary depending on the region.
What kind of shoes are best for capturing French street fashion?
Comfort and style go hand-in-hand here. Classic ballet flats, chic loafers, and stylish ankle boots are all great choices. And if you're feeling fancy, a pair of low heels can definitely add that extra oomph!

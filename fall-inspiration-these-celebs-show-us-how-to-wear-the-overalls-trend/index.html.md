# Fall Inspiration: These Celebs Show Us How to Wear the Overalls Trend ...

## 1. Selena Gomez

![Selena Gomez](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/el/lb/kc6l5qrv_585x883.jpg)

Selena added some instant 'chic' points to her look when she rolled up her overalls and added heels! Embracing the patterned shoe, Selena added some print with her bandeau.

## 2. Alexa Chung

![Alexa Chung](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gt/yb/sx9g87iv_585x895.jpg)

Alexa Chung makes the look fall-friendly by adding a Burberry trench coat and some two-toned flats!

## 3. Emma Watson

![Emma Watson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ll/fa/qch7tjn3_600x900.jpg)

Emma always looks effortlessly classy so, it's no surprise that she managed to make overalls look just great. She paired it with a collared button up underneath, a sleek ponytail, and some black espadrilles!

## 4. Kaite Holmes

![Kaite Holmes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rs/jn/xpscgvoa_435x580.jpg)

Katie gives the trend a bohemian vibe with a printed cross body bag, short overalls, and loose wavy hair.

## 5. Hilary Duff

![Hilary Duff](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zq/hn/ilqe86hv_517x800.jpg)

Hilary's style shows a brand new way to wear overalls! Keep the top half of it down, showcasing your cool tee underneath and making the overalls feel more like jeans.

## 6. Alessandra Ambrosio

![Alessandra Ambrosio](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wc/hu/xeiuf9aw_600x913.jpg)

Alessandra makes casual look sexy. Add a lace crop top underneath your denim overalls and pair it with some converse or Toms!

## 7. Emma Stone

![Emma Stone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r8/s2/bmjy8wx4_600x844.jpg)

Emma wore picked a more loose-fitting overall but by rolling up the bottoms, she gave it a cropped feel and added cute flats!

How are you going to be styling your overalls this fall?

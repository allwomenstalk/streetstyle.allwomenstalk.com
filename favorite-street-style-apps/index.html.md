# 7 Favorite Street Style Apps ...

If you’re looking to up your chic factor, there’s no place better to look – besides here, of course – than a few good street style apps. But there are so many to choose from, how do you know which are worthwhile an which are a waste? Keep reading, loves. I’ve tried loads of them, and I’ve found a few I use nearly every day, for inspiration and salivation (who doesn’t love drooling over a new pair of shoes?). Here are my picks for the best of the best street style apps.

## 1. Fashion Freax Street Style App

_ **On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](http://click.linksynergy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
**For Android at:** play.google.com_.
I love how easy it is to connect Fashion Freax with your other social media streams, like Facebook and Twitter, which is why it tops my list of the best street style apps. I’ve experienced the occasional crash, but it’s so much fun to use this app, I use it almost every day anyway.

## 2. Pose

_ **On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](http://click.linksynergy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
**For Android at:** play.google.com_
If you love your look today – and of course you do! – take a photo and share it on Pose. You’ll be in good company – celebs like Rachel Zoe love Pose – and it’s fun to share your look each day! You can even shop the styles you see on Pose… how convenient!

## 3. Weheartit

![Weheartit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/05/3_weheartit_270x480.jpg)

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](http://click.linksynergy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Though technically this is a social media or photo sharing app, I’m constantly checking Weheartit for style inspiration. There’s a lot of other stuff on Weheart it, but there’s so much fashion, too! See something you like? Heart it!

## 4. Pinterest

![Pinterest](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/05/4_pinterest_270x480.jpg)

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](http://click.linksynergy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
**For Android at:** [play.google.com](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.pinterest)
Some people use Pinterest for fattening dessert recipes and (oddly enough) workout tips, but I use it for fashion reasons only. I keep pinboards of things I must have, in various categories. No matter what time it is, or where I am, I can use the app to find some new inspiration, and share the love with all of my fellow pin addicts.

## 5. Trendabl

![Trendabl](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/05/5_trendabl_270x480.jpg)

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](http://click.linksynergy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Trendabl is sort of an Instragram-type photo sharing app that’s all about fashion (no pictures of people’s pets, kids, or food). Share photos you’ve tagged with your favorite designers, or look around and see who’s wearing your fave labels. I must warn you though: if you thought Pinterest was addictive, you probably shouldn’t even launch Trendabl.

## 6. Snapette

_ **On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](http://click.linksynergy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
**For Android at:** play.google.com_
First of all, I love the name of this app! But I love how it works even more! Wondering what’s hot right now in Milan, Tokyo, Paris, or New York? Then download this app and see what your fellow fashionistas are buying… or maybe share what you’ve just bought!

## 7. AWS App

![AWS App](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/05/7_aws-app_270x480.jpg)

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](http://click.linksynergy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
That’s right! We have our own app you can use to get street style inspiration! If you’re not using it yet, you should be – it’s the best way to get all of our fashion advice, tips and tricks on the go. Stay tuned, loves! There are some incredibly exciting new things coming to our fashion site, and you’ve just got to see them first!

With these splendid street style apps, you’ll be the most stylish gal in town… and who knows? You might even be featured on our street style blog! Which of these street style apps do you think you’ll download first… or which do you already use? Or is there an app I forgot to include? Do tell!

# 7 Gorgeous Spring Street Style Looks ...

With the warm weather months in full swing, I’ve seen more spring street style looks than I can count! From striking floral frocks to casual-cool striped tops, these 7 spring street style stars are turning up the heat.

## 1. Oh, the Places You'll Go

![Oh, the Places You'll Go](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/05/1_oh-the-places-youll-go_600x900.jpg)

As far as spring street style goes, this feminine, put-together look is pretty unbeatable. We adore Style On The Line blogger Sierra's map print Madewell frock (how original), fiery red JCrew blazer, and Zara heels. Work it, girl! I'd rock her relatively affordable look on a spring date outdoors. I might even swap those killer heels for a pair of Chucks to stay cozy.

## 2. Feminine, Floral, and Fabulous!

![Feminine, Floral, and Fabulous!](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/05/2_feminine-floral-and-fabulous_333x500.jpg)

Refinery29 snapped this art director in a springy floral frock from ASOS. If you're wondering how to wear it, try it out at the office --the sleek black tights and shoes keep the ensemble tame enough (no breaking dress code!). Or, try a swingy dress in a similar oversize flower pattern for a casual brunch with the girls for an unforgettable girly-chic look.

## 3. Darling in Polka Dots

![Darling in Polka Dots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/05/3_darling-in-polka-dots_600x899.jpg)

SF Style regularly captures the best dressed of San Francisco on film, and this lovely lady is no exception. This gal's tiered skirt provides a nice contrast to her polka-dotted top. And those gold oxfords add an unexpected touch of flair. Dying to put together a lookalike outfit of your own? Score similar pieces from Forever21, ASOS, H&M, or Zara.

## 4. Queen of the (Concrete) Jungle

![Queen of the (Concrete) Jungle](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/05/4_queen-of-the-concrete-jungle_432x648.jpg)

I've spotted my fair share of New York City gals donning black on black, so it's nice to see this cool chick taking a risk by pairing menswear pieces with a fierce leopard skirt. And she manages to work two of the season's hottest trends at once! Jealous? ASOS, Zara, Forever21, Charlotte Russe, Delia's, and more have similar items for sale --you could even check the men's section at Old Navy or your favorite department store for button down tops and sweaters. Or, you know, just borrow a few pieces from your beau (we won't tell!).

## 5. Wonderfully Whimsical

![Wonderfully Whimsical](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/05/5_wonderfully-whimsical_530x804.jpg)

The Clothes Horse's Rebecca stepped out in a cozy striped top from Urban Outfitters, a thrift store skirt, and a quirky fox purse from Little Odd Forest. This ensemble is playful --without looking childish-- and works with her shaggy Pixie cut. I'd wear it on a coffee date or late-night study session with a cutie from class.

## 6. Let the Sunshine in

![Let the Sunshine in](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/05/6_let-the-sunshine-in_560x484.jpg)

Chilly spring nights, meet your worst enemy: a sunny (and cozy!) yellow sweater. I love how she pairs this knit piece with party-ready sandals and a notice-me metallic clutch. Tres chic! Find similar pieces at Macy's, Target, and ASOS. Who else is planning to wear this on her next dinner-and-a-movie date?

## 7. Pretty in Pastel

![Pretty in Pastel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/05/7_pretty-in-pastel_560x420.jpg)

This style-savvy ensemble proves that pastel can be just as sleek as black. Wear a similar light and bright button front pastel top to the office with your go-to pencil skirt or to a house party with a flirty mini. Forever21 and ASOS are great places to find pastel tops that are both on-trend and affordable. So what are you waiting for?

These are 7 of my favorite spring street style looks. Which ones are you obsessing over? Are there any street style bloggers you’d like to add to the list? Let me know in the comments, fashionistas!

# 8 Graphic Bags to Bring Your Street Style to Life ...

An outfit is only as good as its accessories, right? Well, graphic bags are becoming the next must-have item! It's the perfect way to animate your street style. Check out some of the hottest ones on the market at the moment:

## 1. Marc by Marc Jacobs

![Marc by Marc Jacobs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l7/w3/ie0zcogo_600x482.jpg)

Make a splash in the fashion scene with this Marc by Marc Jacobs Luna Tarp Fergus Tote available on [neimanmarcus.com](http://www.neimanmarcus.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) ($298). I'm obsessed with all these colors!

## 2. Moschino

![Moschino](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bj/tl/v74rvqm8_600x498.jpg)

Stars everywhere have been wearing Moschino! Add a cute and quirky bag to your outfit like Moschino's Spongebob Coated Canvas Bucket Bag, available on [luisaviaroma.com](http://www.luisaviaroma.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) for $950.

## 3. Nasty Gal

I love this Parental Advisory Clutch from [nastygal.com](http://nastygal.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)! It's only $48 and would be a perfect addition to any outfit.

## 4. Stella Mccartney

Star power! Stella McCartney's Cavendish Clutch is the perfect accessory and is available on [stellamccartney.com](http://www.stellamccartney.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) for $785.

## 5. H&m

Sorry to go all Gwen Stefani on you but, it's bananas. B-a-n-a-n-a-s! Use this H&M Canvas Bag to make quite the statement! It's available for $9.95 on [hm.com](http://www.hm.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

## 6. Celine

![Celine](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ta/sh/u42vmo8j_600x483.jpg)

I'm obsessed with this tote! I think the design is awesome! This Born Free Céline Tote Bag is available on [shopbop.com](http://www.shopbop.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) for $225.

## 7. Jeremy Scott Travel

Can't settle on a color? Choose them all with the Longchamp Tote from Jeremy Scott Travel! It's $370 and available on [bloomingdales.com](http://www1.bloomingdales.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

## 8. Alexander Wang

This one's the priciest one on the list but I mean, it's pretty amazing. Alexander Wang's Dumbo Bacpack is available on [shopbop.com](http://www.shopbop.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) for $1,195.

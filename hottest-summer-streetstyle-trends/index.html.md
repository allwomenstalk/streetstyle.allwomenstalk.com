# 8 Hottest Summer Streetstyle Trends ...

With rising temperatures, we're sure you're all aching for a change of wardrobe and all set to flaunt your summer streetstyle trends! Need a little summer inspiration? Fret not, for we have scoured the ends of the internet to find the hottest streetstyle trends this season. From statement stripes to graphic prints to the ever classic little white dress, these trends will have you welcoming the summer in style!

## 1. Statement in Stripes

![Statement in Stripes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/06/1_statement-in-stripes_600x900.jpg)

Stripes just got bigger, bolder and better! Embrace stripes wholeheartedly this summer, à la this off duty model spotted in Paris. We love her oversized Balmain jacket paired with a striped dress (yes, stripes on stripes is fab!) and one can never go wrong with booties. Try Mango or Asos to try this streetstyle trend for less.

## 2. Subtle and Sweet

![Subtle and Sweet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/06/2_sublte-and-sweet_500x750.jpg)

Memorial Day has gone by and it's time to bring out your whites. Melissa of "The Manfattan Project" looked stunning in this Macy's dress paired with Christian Siriano for Payless shoes. An all white look doesn't have to be boring...not at all! Just add an edgy, standout accessory like this Kate Spade necklace and you're set.

## 3. Cosy in a Maxi Dress

![Cosy in a Maxi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/06/3_cosy-in-a-maxi-dress_600x900.jpg)

The maxi trend is not going anywhere this season (Yay!) and we absolutely love the crochet details on Elsa Sylvan's maxi dress. It's comfortable yet chic and we approve wholeheartedly. So don't forget to find a maxi dress with a boho feel and a show stopping twist! Find similar dresses at Free People, Urban Outfitters and Charlotte Russe.

## 4. Oh so Blue!

![Oh so Blue!](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/06/4_oh-so-blue_600x833.jpg)

We love everything about Elena Perminova's outfit, from the chambray crop top to the mirrored blue wayfarers (a must-have this summer) to the cross body bag to the high waisted pants. Cobalt is one of the hottest colors this season and looks oh-so-fabulous when paired with white!

## 5. Blinding Neon

![Blinding Neon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/06/5_blinding-neon_600x900.jpg)

This is one trend that took me a little getting used to, but as a proud owner of a brand new pair of neon yellow pants, I say go for it! You can have SO much fun with neon and isn't that what summer is all about anyway? We love Elann Zelie of elannzelie.com's fearlessness. Who else would have thought of pairing that Lane Bryant blazer with those Lane Bryant pants?!

## 6. Classic Goodness

![Classic Goodness](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/06/6_classic-goodness_497x750.jpg)

A denim shirt is a must have for any season and we love how Carla of thefashionthroughmyeyes.com takes a classic denim on denim look and adds a touch of flair with her Zara scarf and bag. Lesson: Don't be afraid to take on a classic look and make it your own.

## 7. Graphic Content

![Graphic Content](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/06/7_graphic-content_482x724.jpg)

Blogger Jacey Duprie was spotted in this Dior wrap dress at Coachella 2013 and can we just say how badly we want it! Wrap dresses flatter most body types and we love that fun print. So get your print on with similar dresses from JCPenney, Express, Dorothy Perkins and the ever dependable Forever 21. If you're not ready to go all out yet, try a pair of bold, printed shorts with a neutral top instead.

## 8. E for Embellished

_ It’s all about having fun with your sunglasses this summer and these sunglasses seen at Coachella 2013 most definitely fit the bill. While we’re at it, can we also say how much we love those graphic shorts?! Try Forever 21, Asos, Urban Outfitters, Express or better still DIY! Try this look from [cupcakesandcashmere.com](https://cupcakesandcashmere.com/diy/diy-embellished-sunglasses).

Armed with these summer streetstyle trends, we're sure you're all set for a fabulous you, this new season! So, which of these streetstyle trends get a Yay and which ones get a Nay?

# 17 Ideas for Wearing Leggings Once Its Cool out ...

Leggings are a great fall closet staple because there are virtually hundreds of ways to wear them. You can easily combine leggings with warm weather items to take your closet into the next season without spending a bunch of money. Need some ideas? Here are some great inspiration photos to get you started. Have fun with it!

## 1. Add a Sweater and Boots

![clothing, footwear, tights, leather, jeans,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ba/tx/nhu6a3j15857f9716ad89233197250_600x749.jpg)

Leggings look perfect with a long sweater and a pair of boots.

## 2. Wear a Tunic

![clothing, red, dress, footwear, outerwear,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ce/t6/z3n64uwj5857fe2fc4dcf698769055_600x680.jpg)

A great tunic looks great over a pair of leggings. Add heels for work or flats for play.

## 3. Add a Scarf

![clothing, sleeve, outerwear, neck, blouse,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wj/zo/x6ee94lq5857f826eb2cb820751785_600x749.jpg)

Any outfit instantly looks great for cool weather when you add a scarf.

## 4. Go Casual

![clothing, footwear, spring, jogging, fashion,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ye/uh/wsl0qju85857f77c40015584750628_600x638.jpg)

A big bag and a baseball hat make this the perfect casual day outfit.

## 5. Perfect under Shorts

![clothing, footwear, leg, thigh, costume,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ea/j0/rabekl5g5857fe1acbb5e811720216_600x650.jpg)

Sheer leggings are perfect under a pair of summer shorts.

## 6. Tall Boots Are Always a Good Idea

![clothing, black, cap, footwear, outerwear,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j0/tr/u9utru445857f9a072de2634958366_600x749.jpg)

Tall boots always work with a pair of leggings. A jacket and scarf complete this look.

## 7. For the Office

![clothing, black, footwear, outerwear, pattern,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mg/m0/jrspfxk05857f9d32d0a3763622965_600x637.jpg)

Flats and a cute sweater make leather leggings perfect for the office.

## 8. Paired with a Basic Black Jacket

![clothing, black, red, snapshot, road,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bv/p7/zfu8m01l5857f9fed64e4797823404_600x729.jpg)

Doesn't this look fabulous? The leggings and the jacket are the same color, leaving you the option of topping the whole thing with a eye catching scarf.

## 9. Top with a Flannel

![clothing, red, footwear, outerwear, spring,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yu/fv/akksnnjm5857fc675a941781009247_600x749.jpg)

A flannel shirt always looks good with leggings and is a great choice for cool days.

## 10. Totally Warm

![clothing, footwear, fashion, leg, spring,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rv/mr/s8xvfrso5857fb0c4a03d479690620_600x749.jpg)

Getting out and about in leggings is always trendy when you look like this.

## 11. Go Colorful

![clothing, fashion, shopping, footwear,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hv/ej/i5omnedt585802438b11d269762828_600x596.jpg)

You can totally rock your brightly colored leggings when the weather is cold. Cute, right?

## 12. Long Sleeves and Heels

![black, clothing, footwear, human positions, lady,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/to/jt/qnr4tofk5857faac50a80349378256_600x721.jpg)

A long sleeved shirt that's longer in the back is ideal for pairing with leggings.

## 13. Go All Black

![black, clothing, footwear, leather, jacket,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gu/dj/gyg1otp85857f96006309775346417_600x749.jpg)

Leather leggings are perfect for an all black look.

## 14. Bright Shoes

![white, black, footwear, clothing, fashion,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n5/km/d71dgu8e5857fbd605f3c235169235_600x699.jpg)

Rock your fall leggings with a fab pair of brightly colored booties.

## 15. With a Denim Jacket

Nothing is better in the fall than a great jean jacket with your leggings.

## 16. Try Some Pattern

![clothing, pattern, denim, outerwear, fashion,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hr/df/m0o1ll855857fadf9b273273130711_600x600.jpg)

Your patterned leggings look just as good with a sweater as they do with a tank top.

## 17. For a Day out

![clothing, footwear, fashion, outerwear, leather,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zz/nf/eix4emvr5857fa9870e1d439257577_600x719.jpg)

A pair of comfy shoes and a sweatshirt make your leggings perfect for cool weather and a casual day.

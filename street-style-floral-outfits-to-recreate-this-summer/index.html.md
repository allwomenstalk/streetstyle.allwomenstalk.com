# 9 Street Style Floral Outfits to Recreate This Summer ...

There are so many different street style floral outfits that are worth recreating this summer! If you ask me, you can never go wrong with floral. It's a must-have pattern in your closet! There are so many different looks that you can recreate whether you're trying to look girly or super edgy. Check out some of my favorite street style floral outfits and then let me know which ones you'd like to recreate.

## 1. Rocker Chic

![Rocker Chic](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ri/d4/u4jtolpi_600x880.jpg)

You'd be surprised at how easily you can make floral look rocker chic. Pair a simple skirt (like this one from [hm.com](http://www.hm.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)) with a black/grey crop top or muscle tank. Of course, no rocker look is complete without leather! You can incorporate this with a leather backpack ([farfetch.com](http://www.farfetch.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com); perfect to keep for back to school!) To complete the look, wear combat boots! This is just one of many street style floral outfits to recreate this summer.

## 2. Flared Pants

![Flared Pants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jc/eb/kqfofm2l_500x750.jpg)

I love flared pants! They're such a light and breezy option for the summer. I think they're great to throw on at the beach with a crop top! You can find some affordable options on [pacsun.com](http://www.pacsun.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

## 3. Blouse + Boyfriend Jeans

![Blouse + Boyfriend Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fj/mv/wi5wwrti_580x871.jpg)

Boyfriend jeans are always a good idea. Pair a floral blouse with a pair and you'll look totally chic! I love the distressed boyfriend jeans from [nastygal.com](http://www.nastygal.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com). You can wear a vibrant top to add some color! Throw on a floppy hat and rock some nude pumps to really take the outfit to the next level.

## 4. Romper

![Romper](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l5/bs/seuoci4p_585x893.jpg)

Rompers are my one of my go-to items in the summer time. It's casual, stylish, and totally worth stocking up on. I'm obsessed with the ones from [urbanoutfitters.com](http://www.urbanoutfitters.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com); I like that they're all very boho-chic. Since it's a casual look, I would wear some sandals and carry a small cross body bag! That would complete the whole look.

## 5. Patterned Jeans

![Patterned Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rs/ki/wke1bfid_600x880.jpg)

Patterned jeans are such an awesome way to add a little pizazz to your outfit. I love the Victorian Floral jeans from [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com). With colorful flowers on your pants, try and keep the rest of the look simple. Wear a blouse that coordinates with one of the colors and then you're all set! Would you wear floral jeans?

## 6. Casual

![Casual](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n7/vu/hxzntt6o_375x563.jpg)

An easy way to wear the floral trend is with a casual summer dress. You can find tons of those on [forever21.com](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com). If you want to add some edge to the look, pair it with some combat boots and a leather jacket. That's how I would wear it! If you want to keep things sweet and girly, pair it with nice sandals and some oversized sunglasses!

## 7. Jumpsuit

![Jumpsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qf/bg/mj4u9pty_521x796.jpg)

I am obsessed with jumpsuits! I think it's such a easy way to look stylish while feeling comfortable and casual. Since it's already a statement piece, try and keep the accessories to a minimum. A thin belt would be a nice addition, paired with some matching oxford flats! I love the jumpsuits from [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/tzXjv8).. because they're really colorful and sure to turn heads wherever you go!

## 8. Blazer

![Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o5/rb/k6u6v1jl_500x750.jpg)

I think floral blazers are a must-have item in every girl's closet. It's great to have whenever you need to throw something over your outfit and add in a pop of color. I would definitely pair my floral blazer with an all-black outfit! You can find some cool blazers on [hm.com](http://www.hm.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com). What would you wear it with?

## 9. Graphic Tee + Skirt

![Graphic Tee + Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mh/gk/ssdo9wrg_533x800.jpg)

I love the idea of pairing a graphic tee with a floral skirt. I think it adds a little edge to your girly outfit! For example, you can pair your floral skirt with your favorite band tank, or any of the adorable (and affordable) options from [forever21.com](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com). I also the love the mixing & matching of patterns because it makes the outfit more vibrant and expressive!

Well, there you have it. These are just 9 out of many awesome street style floral outfits to recreate this summer! Of the list, which look was your favorite? How do YOU love to wear the floral trend?

# 7 Street Style Inspired Ways to Wear a Suit ...

There are plenty of different ways to wear a suit, if a quick look through street style images is anything to go by. All over the world, style savvy girls are embracing this masculine trend. From printed suit sets to breezy shorts suits, the suit is definitely having a trending moment right now. Check out these street style snaps for ideas on different ways to wear a suit.

## 1. Basic Black Suit

![Basic Black Suit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/06/1_basic-black-suit_592x888.jpg)

One of the simplest ways to wear a suit is to stick to basics. And you can’t get any more basic than a classic black suit. Ilona Hamer was photographed by Candice Lake in Sydney wearing a classic black suit with a slouchy white t-shirt and strappy heels. While she’s wearing head to toe designer, you can easily replicate this look on a budget. The key to making a suit look a million bucks is to make sure that you get one that fits just right.

## 2. Patterned Trouser Suit

![Patterned Trouser Suit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/06/2_patterned-trouser-suit_592x888.jpg)

Prints and patterns are everywhere right now. Namwan Satidthong was snapped by Glamour Magazine wearing this bold blue patterned suit. The oversized style of the suit, combined with the patent oxford shoes, gives off some definite Annie Hall vibes. The leopard print detail on her purse is also a quirky touch.

## 3. Graphic Print Suit

![Graphic Print Suit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/06/3_graphic-print-suit_592x888.jpg)

Zahra Lyla Pedram was shot by Suzanne Middlemass in London wearing this graphic Clover Canyon suit. Designers are loving digital prints right now, and the possibilities are endless when it comes to designs. With such an eye-catching print on the suit, Zahra kept the rest of her outfit fairly simple with a plain black top, black Zara bag, and simple strappy heels.

## 4. Pastel Pink Suit

![Pastel Pink Suit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/06/4_pastel-pink-suit_592x888.jpg)

Give a masculine look a very feminine twist by choosing a suit in a pastel colour. Nicole Huisman was snapped by Glamour Magazine wearing this pastel pink Bruuns Bazaar suit. She teams it with a plain grey t-shirt and black patent loafers. The bright red bag adds a fun touch to the entire look.

## 5. Man-Style Suit

![Man-Style Suit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/06/5_man-style-suit_592x888.jpg)

Most suits these days tend to be slim-fitting. But if you’re after a different way to wear this trend, take a leaf out of Peony Lim’s book and choose a baggier style of suit. She was snapped by Candice Lake in Paris wearing this vintage Yves Saint Laurent suit. She accessorises her suit with a masculine tie, felt hat, and oversized clutch. Stiletto heels and bright red lipstick add a feminine touch to her look.

## 6. Shorts Suit

![Shorts Suit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/06/6_shorts-suit_600x902.jpg)

Shorts suits are perfect for summer dressing. Here, Zina from Fashionvibe stands out in a brightly coloured Gina Tricot shorts suit. She teams it with a Zara crop top, Miu Miu clutch, and black chunky heels. The reflective sunglasses are a funky addition to the outfit. This shorts suit would also look great teamed with a tucked-in blouse or a patterned tee.

## 7. Metallic Suit

![Metallic Suit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/06/7_metallic-suit_432x648.jpg)

Get a little bit futuristic with metallics this season. This stylish lady was shot by Kelly Stuart for Elle Magazine. She wears a sleek metallic suit teamed with a very appropriate Star Wars t-shirt. A blue clutch and red sunglasses make for colourful accessories, while plain black heels anchor the entire look. A metallic suit like this would be great for evening wear, teamed with a slinky blouse and costume jewellery.

These are just a few street style-inspired ways to wear a suit. What do you think of the matching suit trend? What would be your tips for pulling off this look?

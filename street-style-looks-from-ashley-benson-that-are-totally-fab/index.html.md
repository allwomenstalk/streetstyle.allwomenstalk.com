# 7 Street Style Looks from Ashley Benson That Are Totally Fab ...

There are so many fantastic street style looks from Ashley Benson. Her character on Pretty Little Liars, Hanna Marin, is always dressed to the nines, wearing designer-brands but Ashley and her character are total opposites! Sure, Ashley gets super glammed-up for red-carpet events, but when it comes to just running errands, she's dressed like you and me. I love that she dresses for comfort, but still looks stylish 24/7. Check out some of my favorite street style looks from Ashley Benson!

## 1. Comfy Stylish

![Comfy Stylish](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dt/xv/a5ogcktm_600x780.jpg)

This is a really easy look to recreate. Rock some distressed jeans, throw on an oversized flannel, floppy hat, and you're good to go. For shoes, you can rock ankle boots or combats! You'll feel comfy all day while still earning major style points. This is just one of the cool looks from Ashley Benson!

## 2. Graphic Tees

![Graphic Tees](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eg/n1/o1kwx627_600x943.jpg)

Ashley's always sporting graphic tees. They're usually her favorite bands such as Nirvana. I think it's a really cool look because it's expressive and stylish all at once. You can rock your graphic tees with denim cut-offs or black skinnies!

## 3. Rebel Chic

![Rebel Chic](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uu/ht/zghebtbe_600x900.jpg)

No one does rebel chic like Benson does. Her style at last year's Coachella festival was one of my favorites. She stayed comfortable while still managing to look totally stylish. It's really easy to recreate this look - just rock some ripped jeans, your favorite graphic tee, and throw on your old converse. I love that Ashley's wardrobe is made up of things that I can find in my own closet!

## 4. Leather + Patterns

![Leather + Patterns](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xx/ah/hvguix56_500x682.jpg)

I love it "rocker" meets "girly". Ashley rocks this look effortlessly. A fun way to have the best of both worlds is to rock a black crop top and pair it with some floral (or any other fun print) high-waisted shorts. Throw your leather jacket over it and don't forget your over-sized sunglasses! It's the perfect look for spring. Would you rock the edgy-meets-girly look or would you rather have one or the other?

## 5. Casual

![Casual](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k9/ul/yhbhovlt_600x840.jpg)

Unlike her Pretty Little Liars character, Ashley's always dressed for comfort. She always keeps things casual by rocking some denim, ankle boots, and a plain white tee. Of course, it wouldn't be her signature look if she didn't throw her leather jacket over it! This look is perfect for running errands, hanging with your gal pals, and whatever else the day might bring you.

## 6. Pretty in Pink

![Pretty in Pink](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/no/th/z2yrwe3r_500x800.jpg)

I think Ashley looks gorgeous in "girly" colors. I like that she rocks hi-low dresses and pairs it with a sweater on top. It gives this "effortlessly cool" vibe to the outfit, which I love! To achieve this look, you can find hi-low skirts/dresses from Forever 21. Then, just throw an off-the-shoulder sweater on top of it. For shoes, rock some high-heeled lace-up booties!

## 7. Knee-high Boots

![Knee-high Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vk/bx/ejnjq23u_456x640.jpg)

Ashley's always rocking converse or combat boots, so it's nice to see when she swaps them for something else. This is actually one of my favorite looks from her because she manages to stay casual and comfy, yet totally fashionable. To achieve this look yourself, wear some dark skinny jeans, a loose fitting tee (a graphic one if you want some "edge" to your outfit), and then rock your knee-high boots. You'll look very stylish!

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of many fabulous street style looks from Pretty Little Liars' Ashley Benson. She's become quite the fashionista, so I'm sure there'll be more and more amazing looks. Of the list, which street style look is your favorite? Will you be recreating any of these outfits this spring?

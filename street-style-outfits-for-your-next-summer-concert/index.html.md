# 7 Street Style Outfits for Your Next Summer Concert ...

There are so many fantastic outfits for a summer concert. Whether you'll be outside at a music festival or indoors at an intimate venue, you always have to look your best. After all, you never know if you might run into the band before/after the show! Check out some of the cool outfits for a summer concert and then let me know which looks your favorites are.

## 1. Bandeau + High-waisted Shorts

![Bandeau + High-waisted Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lh/w7/elknotaz_500x750.jpg)

Bandeaus have become a huge trend this summer. You can pair them with some high-waisted shorts! I love the lace bandeaus (try [American Eagle](https://www.ae.com/us/en)) because they add a cute and girly touch to the outfit. Of course, if you think bandeaus leave you too exposed, you can wear yours underneath a sheer top or muscle tank! This is just one of many outfits for a summer concert.

## 2. Band Tee

![Band Tee](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ws/dv/vusabr3v_427x640.jpg)

What better way to show your love for music than with a band t-shirt? It's a cool, casual outfit that'll make you look totally stylish at the concert. If the band you're going to see has their own merchandise, you can even wear that! You never know, the band might see you in the crowd and shout you out for wearing their stuff! Which band tees do you own?

## 3. Crop Top + Midi

![Crop Top + Midi](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j3/o5/bh99wu0m_520x800.jpg)

I think pairing a crop top and a midi skirt is the perfect balance between sexy and modest. The crop top allows you to bare some skin while the midi skirt keeps you covered up! I think it's a really boho-chic look and would be perfect for any upcoming music festivals. Recently, I've fallen in love with tie-dye cropped tops and you can find some at [Eeropostale](https://www.aeropostale.com/). To complete the whole boho look, put your hair in a braid!

## 4. Kimono Cardigan

![Kimono Cardigan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fj/ix/kkyax2rc_400x600.jpg)

If you're rocking a crop top but don't want to look/feel 'too' exposed, throw a kimono cardigan over it. You can choose between tons of cute ones over at [Forever 21](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com). I'd go with a floral pattern just because I'm floral-obsessed and think it's perfect for the summer.

## 5. All Black

![All Black](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cj/jb/vb0n6uiw_600x900.jpg)

Summer doesn't mean you need to hide all your black clothing. All-black ensembles are always fashionable! You can rock a black romper (try [H&M](http://www.hm.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)) or even a jumpsuit. If you want to add some color to your outfit, do it through your accessories!

## 6. Summer Dress

_ An easy choice for a summer concert is a good ol' summer dress. It's stylish but still really casual. There are so many adorable (and affordable) options that you can find at Forever 21_. It's light and breezy, making it perfect for the warm weather!

## 7. Fringes

![Fringes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v6/l8/u2dimu3j_500x750.jpg)

Fringes are a summer must-have; I'm obsessed with them! I think they're such a fun and youthful addition to any outfit. For concerts, you can incorporate fringes on your clothes (try this fringed dress from [Top Shop](https://goo.gl/mSWbP3)), on your cross body bag ([Neiman Marcus](http://www.neimanmarcus.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)), or even on your shoes. You can also turn this into a DIY (do it yourself) project and fringe one of your maxi skirts or dresses!

Well, there you have it. These are just seven out of many awesome street style outfits to create for your next summer concert! Concerts are all about expression, so try and reflect that in your outfit. Of the list, which look is your favorite? Which look will you be trying to recreate, and for what concert?

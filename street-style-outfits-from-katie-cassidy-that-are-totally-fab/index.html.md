# 7 Street Style Outfits from Katie Cassidy That Are Totally Fab ...

There are so many gorgeous street style outfits from Katie Cassidy that'll make you wish you could take a trip to her closet! I'm such a huge fan of her and her sense of style. She always looks great - no matter if it's at a formal event or just running errands. Did you know that she even has her own fashion blog? It's called "Tomboy KC" and it's pretty awesome. Check out some of my favorite street style outfits from Katie Cassidy.

## 1. Boyfriend Jeans

![Boyfriend Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/og/oo/dw65y0ok_600x896.jpg)

Katie definitely knows how to rock the boyfriend jeans. I love how she dressed them up by wearing pointy-toed heels and a plaid sweater over a collared shirt. Somehow, she makes the boyfriend jeans look very polished. This outfit would be perfect for running errands around the city or meeting up with your friends for a night out. This is just one of the outfits from Katie Cassidy that look fabulous.

## 2. Casual

![Casual](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/aj/dd/cutnfwmb_358x537.jpg)

I wish my "casual" attire looked like this! She rocks a plain white tee, dark skinnies, and some insanely cute cheetah-printed booties. The big scarf is perfect for off-again, on-again "spring" weather! Of course, those shades take the outfit to next level.

## 3. Oversize Sweater Dress

![Oversize Sweater Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j5/j3/ev2mlqf5_402x604.jpg)

Ok, I'm kinda obsessed with this look. How gorgeous is that dress?! It's totally casual but still effortlessly chic. I'm also a big fan of that Henri Bendel cross body bag! The messy bun and oversized round shades are perfect with this look. P.S I love that she chose to wear combat boots with this outfit. They always add major style points.

## 4. Colorblock Sweater

![Colorblock Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b2/tq/hx98101g_600x900.jpg)

You can never go wrong with some color block, right? You can find a similar sweeter on [scoopnyc.com](https://goo.gl/4OdmsH) Then just pair that sweater with some ripped skinnies. You can find similar styled jeans at [rag-bone.com](http://www.rag-bone.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com). Throw on some oversized sunglasses and a hat and you're good to go.

## 5. Printed Pants

![Printed Pants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nm/o7/lgu6ob8b_600x913.jpg)

I love this look. It screams sophisticated and chic! I love that she kept the colors very neutral. The black, grey, and beige work very well together. You might want to lose the coat for the warm weather, but the beige clutch will make up for it!

## 6. Leather

![Leather](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zi/ew/y6g8vxlz_600x900.jpg)

I'm pretty much in love with any look that incorporates leather into the outfit. This outfit has a total "bad-ass" vibe to it. Am I right, ladies? I'm loving the black knee-high boots. You'll be strutting your stuff, looking like you're ready to take over the world! You can pair your leather skirt with a basic white tee/sweater, a colorful/printed one, or a graphic tee. - basically, anything that looks great with leather.

## 7. Menswear Inspired

![Menswear Inspired](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u3/s5/sir6wwan_600x896.jpg)

Katie is definitely big on wearing menswear-inspired clothing. After all, her blog is called "Tomboy KC" for a reason! I love this look. You can find a similar shirt on [vince.com](http://www.vince.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com). The beanie is from [hatattack.com](http://www.hatattack.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com), and is so cute. I love the white denim - it pairs really nice with her shirt. This outfit is perfect for running errands and staying comfy and casual.

Well, there you have it. Who knew this Arrow star was such a fashionista? Make sure you check out her blog ([tomboykc.com](http://tomboykc.com/)), you'll find tons of inspiration there! Of the list, which look is your favorite? How would you recreate these outfits in your own way?

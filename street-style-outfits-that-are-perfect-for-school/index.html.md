# 7 Street Style Outfits That Are Perfect for School ...

There are so many fantastic street style outfits for school! If I had a bit more time in the mornings, I'd probably be more of a fashionista than I really am. Instead, I 'snooze' my alarm clock too many times (can you blame me, though?) and end up wearing sweatpants everywhere - LOL! This list will hopefully inspire you with new outfit ideas and you can set up your outfit the night before. That way, even if you hit snooze on that wakeup call, you'll still be able to look good! Here's some of the cool street style outfits for school:

## 1. Casual

_ I'm the type of person that likes to keep things easy and casual. So, my go-to outfit for class is some classic skinny jeans, a basic tank top, a cardigan, and some fun printed flats. Of course, there are many ways this look can be achieved! For the jeans, you can opt for distressed ones, boyfriend ones, cropped ones, whichever you desire! For cardigans, I love the ones from forever21.com_. Who said you can't be casual AND stylish at the same time?! This is just one of many street style outfits for school.

## 2. Black & White

![Black & White](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mw/lz/nx2fptzf_600x900.jpg)

Black and white ensembles are always a good idea. They're classic and timeless so you can never go wrong. I also feel like this color combo makes you look/feel sleek, sexy, and sophisticated! While the weather's still nice, rock a white skirt and pair it with a black blouse. For an edgier vibe, swap the two and rock a black leather skirt paired with a white top! How would you style these two colors?

## 3. Rocker Chic

![Rocker Chic](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xn/sm/uc1uhfuv_600x924.jpg)

Be expressive with your clothes and show everyone just how creative and imaginative you really are. Pair your favorite band tee (you should have a stack of these in your drawer!) with some dark-wash jeans. For a pop of color, try some printed Doc Martens from [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com). To top off the look, I'd throw on a military or denim jacket. This look will make you appear effortlessly cool.

## 4. Punk Glam

![Punk Glam](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wh/qe/ro7rlcsw_600x900.jpg)

Yup, being punk and glamorous at the same time is an actual possibility. Try tartan pants to add some cool style points to your outfit and then pair it with all-black pieces like ankle booties, a leather jacket, and even a beanie. The pop of color in your pants is the perfect balance between punk and glam!

## 5. Overalls

_ If you want to look cutesy for school, overall dresses are the way to go. I love the floral printed one from forever21.com_! You can then add some edge to your look by wearing combat boots or adding a leather cross body bag.

## 6. Denim on Denim

![Denim on Denim](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oy/p3/oxxskk07_584x876.jpg)

I love the denim on denim look. I think it's really easy to pull off if you add some type of color and/or print to the look. For example, if you're rocking two shades of denim, add some print on your shoe. It'll keep your outfit from looking too 'overwhelming.'

## 7. Cozy

![Cozy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t2/g7/wzhpq4mv_585x600.jpg)

Choosing comfort does not mean you're choosing it over style. It's easy to look comfortable and like a fashionista. Pair some skinny jeans with an oversized sweater. The sweater will make you feel cozy all day long! You can make the look feel a bit more polished by adding a collared shirt underneath the sweater.

Well, there you have it. These are just seven out of many awesome street style outfits for school! They're all perfect for the fall season/weather. Of the list, which look is your favorite? Which look will you be recreating?

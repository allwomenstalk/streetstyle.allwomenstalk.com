# 7 Street Style Outfits with Harem Pants to Recreate ...

There are so many awesome street style outfits with harem pants that you can recreate this fall! The trend has really spiked in popularity and there are so many ways that you can dress it up or down. Whether you're pairing it with a tailored leather jacket, bulky sweater, or a denim top, you can make the look unique and true to your style. Don't shy away from them just because you think you can't "pull it off"! After all, you can't knock it till you try it! Here are some street style outfits with harem pants to recreate:

## 1. Casual

![Casual](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qm/qf/vgmooqf8_600x900.jpg)

If you like to lounge around in casual pieces - and who doesn't? - then you can totally recreate this look. Just pair some basic black harem pants (like these from [hm.com](http://www.hm.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)) with a loose tee like this draped tunic from [urbanoutfitters.com](http://www.urbanoutfitters.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com). For shoes, some flats will give you a classic touch. This is just one of many street style outfits with harem pants that you can recreate!

## 2. Printed

![Printed](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u7/qk/fckhwomq_600x801.jpg)

Prints are always a good idea! They're very expressive and can take any outfit to the next level. Even though it's now fall, you can still keep things tropical with these adorable tropical print pants from [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/91lqUf). I would pair them with a crop top and finish the look off with a jacket or cardigan. Which kind of print would YOU like?

## 3. High-waisted

_ Make harem pants the focal point of your outfit by wearing them high-waisted. Adding a belt is a cute touch that will really help draw attention to your outfit! I'm obsessed with these Abstract Windowpane printed pants from forever21.com_! You can pair them with a white tank or crop top, a white belt, and some cute ankle boots. It's a perfect fall outfit for running errands or going out to dinner with your girlfriends!

## 4. Oversized

![Oversized](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yh/zs/nse81nie_600x905.jpg)

Harem pants are known for their bagginess. If you really want to make a fashion statement, pair the oversized pants with an oversized sweater and bulky scarf. It'll definitely add some dimension to your outfit and if you wear it with confidence, you'll always look great.

## 5. Business Chic

_ Who knew harem pants could be so versatile? You can dress them up and make them office-appropriate for work! Try these pleated knit pants from forever21.com_ - the neutral color will instantly add a sophisticated vibe to your look. Add a skinny belt on your waist, tuck in a white blouse, and throw on a blazer! Ta-da, totally [business chic](https://streetstyle.allwomenstalk.com/street-style-ways-to-look-business-chic/).

## 6. Boho Chic

![Boho Chic](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yk/oy/vmm1upob_600x900.jpg)

Floppy hats are definitely an item from your summer wardrobe that you should be transitioning into your fall wardrobe! Take the printed look from #2 and add bohemian accessories like a long necklace, bangles, and a hat!

## 7. Pair It with Denim

![Pair It with Denim](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uu/fr/ubwf6392_500x749.jpg)

I would've never thought of pairing harem pants with a denim top but it works so perfectly! It just goes to prove that denim really does look good with anything and everything. Keep the outfit "sleek and polished" by pairing your denim shirt with white harem pants (like these from [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/IgWW0s))! You can add a pop of color to your look with a bold cross body and some flats or heels!

Well, there you have it. These are just seven out of many awesome street style outfits with harem pants to recreate! I'm a big fan of this trend because you can really make it your own. Of the list, which look is your favorite? Which outfit would you recreate?

# 7 Street Style Outfits with Oxfords to Recreate ...

There are so many street style outfits with oxfords that are worth recreating this summer. I think oxfords are a great addition to any summer outfit; it's the perfect way to channel your inner Taylor Swift. I mean, have you seen how many awesome outfits she's rocked with them?! Check out some of the adorable street style outfits with oxfords that are worth recreating!

## 1. Leather

_ Just because summer's here, it doesn't mean you have to stop wearing leather! You can still add an instant "edginess" to your outfit by throwing on a leather jacket over a summer dress (you can browse the collection on forever21.com_). I would go with brown or black oxfords just because they match anything! This is just one of many street style outfits with oxfords that are worth recreating this summer.

## 2. Casual

![Casual](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s2/vz/tpl9n769_470x733.jpg)

For me, a casual outfit for the summer includes a graphic top and dark wash skinny jeans. I always wear it with black oxfords because I think they look great with any color! You can find some awesome black oxfords on [nastygal.com](http://www.nastygal.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com). Since the shoes are black, you can accessorize your outfit with some colorful jewelry or a colorful cross body bag to add some pizazz.

## 3. Black & White

![Black & White](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qx/ur/pat2uciy_600x900.jpg)

I'm a big fan all-black and all-white ensembles. Therefore, I love the idea of pairing the two colors together. Black and white make any outfit look classic and timeless. To recreate this look, I'd go with a black dress (or black top paired with a black skirt) and pair it with some black and white oxfords. You can find some of those on [wildfang.com](http://www.wildfang.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)!

## 4. Romper

_ Another cute outfit to recreate this summer is a patterned romper paired with a flowy cardigan. You can find tons of different printed rompers on forever21.com_. Then, just throw on a cardigan and match your oxfords to it. I personally love the color beige (who doesn't, right?) You can find some cool (and affordable) ones on [charlotterusse.com](http://www.charlotterusse.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com). Which color would you go for?

## 5. Florals

_ If you ask me, you can never go wrong with floral. It's one of my go-to prints in the summer time! For a casual look, pair a floral blouse (you can find those on [usa.tommy.com](http://usa.tommy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)) with some dark wash skinny jeans. Accessorize with a brown leather bag and some brown leather oxfords to match (try forever21.com_).

## 6. Stockings + Shorts

![Stockings + Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ks/ca/0jdyua2t_500x754.jpg)

I've definitely seen this look make it's way back into the fashion scene. The fun thing about pairing stockings and denim shorts is that you can add major style with your stockings. Look through the [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/CBsI4w) collection (which includes tons of different patterns) for an easy way to complete your outfit. For the shoes, I'd go with some black or brown leather oxfords.

## 7. Metallic

![Metallic](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qa/xb/i37lit7i_500x843.jpg)

One easy way to add some pizazz to your outfit is to do it through your shoes. Metallic oxfords will give your outfit some serious style points! You can find tons of different options on [dillards.com](http://www.dillards.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com). Since the shoes will most likely become the focal point of your outfit, I suggest keeping the accessories to a minimum.

Well, there you have it. These are just seven out of many awesome street style outfits with oxfords! Of the list, which outfit was your favorite? Which look will you be recreating?

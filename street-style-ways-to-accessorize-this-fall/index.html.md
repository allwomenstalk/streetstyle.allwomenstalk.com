# 7 Street Style Ways to Accessorize This Fall ...

There are so many awesome street style ways to accessorize for fall. It's one of my favorite seasons for fashion because of layers, layers, layers. Am I right, ladies? Of course, there are other things you can do to look fabulous this season. Check out some cool ways to accessorize for fall and then let me know how much of a fashionista you are!

## 1. Chunky Necklaces

_ One of my favorite ways to accessorize is through jewelry, of course! I love layering a chunky necklace over a knitted sweater. The necklace can be a major statement piece and be the focal point of your outfit! Try forever21.com_ for your necklace needs. This is just one of many ways to accessorize for fall.

## 2. Cozy Scarf

![clothing,footwear,jeans,denim,pattern,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e1/af/ug2j7_clothing_footwear_jeans_denim_pattern_600x880.jpg)

What's fall fashion without a big cozy scarf? It's a great statement piece and all the while, it's keeping you nice and warm. I love infinity scarves because they're so easy to wear and come in so many different patterns and colors! You can find printed scarves on [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com). Their collection offers cute AND affordable options!

## 3. Floppy Hat

![clothing,denim,outerwear,cap,fashion accessory,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dk/r7/mpelu_clothing_denim_outerwear_cap_fashion-accessory_600x900.jpg)

Floppy hats are a big YES this fall! I think they're such a cute addition to any outfit. I especially love when they're paired with some skinny jeans and a knitted sweater! It gives your outfit just the right amount of edge that you're looking for. You can find the perfect floppy hat on [nastygal.com](http://www.nastygal.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)!

## 4. Layers

![clothing,footwear,brown,fashion,tights,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ol/ga/p3wa3_clothing_footwear_brown_fashion_tights_456x856.jpg)

The best part about fall? Rocking layers, of course! I love being able to throw on an oversized sweater over a tank top and some jeans. It gives you the feeling of comfort while still keeping you stylish. The awesome thing about layering is that there's no wrong way to do it. You can have fun with it and mix and match some of your favorite pieces!

## 5. Printed Cardigan

_ Make a fashion statement with a printed knitted cardigan! It's sure to grab people's attention and make you feel like you're wrapped up in a cozy blanket all at the same time. Win/win! You can look through the collection of cardigans on forever21.com_. There are so many cool colors to choose from! It's the perfect thing to throw over a casual outfit.

## 6. Leg Warmers

![clothing,knitting,outerwear,footwear,sweater,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lt/dz/ovxk9_clothing_knitting_outerwear_footwear_sweater_480x750.jpg)

It's time to get the leg warmers out! This makes me incredibly happy because I love this adorable trend. You can pair your leg warmers (try [store.americanapparel.net](https://goo.gl/zr2Z5P) colorful collection) with some knee-high boots, UGGs, and even combat boots! I think it adds a feminine touch to your outfit and will keep you feeling cozy and comfy all day long.

## 7. Pop of Color

![hair,clothing,lady,dress,hairstyle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u9/dt/smqm1_hair_clothing_lady_dress_hairstyle_507x760.jpg)

Just because summer's over, that doesn't mean you have to put the bright-colored clothes away! Keep the color trend alive by using them to spruce up your outfit. Add a pop of color through your shoes (try [converse.com](http://www.converse.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)), through your clothes, or through your jewelry! Looking at these bright and warm colors will remind you of summer and help you get through these chilly days to come.

Well, there you have it. These are just seven out of many awesome street style ways to accessorize this fall! Remember, you can never go wrong with some layers and some confidence. Of the list, which look is your favorite? How will you be accessorizing this season?

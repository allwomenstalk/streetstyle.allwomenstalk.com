# 7 Street Style Ways to Channel Gossip Girl This Fall ...

**Attention all fashionistas:** there are SO many ways you can channel Gossip Girl in your wardrobe this fall. Characters like Blair Waldorf, Serena Van Der Woodsen and even Jenny Humphrey have inspired thousands of girls when it comes to personal style. There's always something so chic about these girls and there are many different ways that you can wear these Gossip Girl staples. Here are some of the cool ways to channel Gossip Girl in your wardrobe:

## 1. Georgina's Statement Scarf

![Georgina's Statement Scarf](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ma/o6/bfhnlzlo_373x560.jpg)

Accessories can make or break an outfit. Georgina Sparks knew the value of a good scarf! You can find tons of cool patterned scarves on [hm.com](http://www.hm.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com). Of course, if you want to channel Serena Van Der Woodsen, go for her signature Burberry scarf ([us.burberry.com](http://us.burberry.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com))! Scarves are a must-have for the fall - how else are you going to keep that neck of yours looking stylish and warm? This is just one of many ways to channel Gossip Girl in your fall looks.

## 2. Blair's Cape/jacket

![Blair's Cape/jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nj/c5/ntdc301f_380x560.jpg)

I'll be honest, I'm STILL obsessed with Blair's green cape jacket that she wore in season 3. I think it's so unique! It's sleek and sophisticated and perfect for a girly look. You can recreate a similar style with the fleece cape jacket from [store.americanapparel.net](https://goo.gl/7nla1Q). There are tons of different colors to choose from!

## 3. Serena's Trench Coat

![Serena's Trench Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jy/ea/jhjxlglx_415x594.jpg)

Serena always had the cutest jackets. A good trench coat is a must-have in every girl's closet! I love the double-breasted belted trench coat from [macys.com](http://www1.macys.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com). The color would look great with anything and everything!

## 4. Jenny's Rocker Style

![Jenny's Rocker Style](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w2/b9/d7ncp9zc_600x845.jpg)

Little J sure did have a fashion transformation throughout the series! She ditched her girly-girl looks early on in the show for an edgier, rock-n-roll type of style. I loved it because if anyone call pull that look off, it's Taylor Momsen. If you want to dress like Jenny Humphrey- channel your inner rockstar. Wear some patterned tights (I love the floral ones from [forever21.com](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)) and pair them with a dress or shirt + skirt combo. With it being fall and all, don't forget to add some layers! An oversized jacket and a scarf will keep you feeling nice and cozy.

## 5. Blair's Preppy Chic Look

![Blair's Preppy Chic Look](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/os/m1/c7b8ka5y_500x761.jpg)

Blair's school outfits always had me wishing that my school required uniforms. That's not weird, right? I mean, she just always looked so chic! To recreate this kind of look, pair a sleek white button-up with a pleated skirt like this plaid one from [urbanoutfitters.com](http://www.urbanoutfitters.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

## 6. Mix and Match

![Mix and Match](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tb/yk/lcdk2l6w_460x647.jpg)

Being a fashionista means being fearless. Don't be afraid to mix and match different colors and patterns! As long as the colors coordinate, you can't go wrong. Take a lesson from Blair's work attire.

## 7. Casual Chic

![Casual Chic](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yl/vl/ask5zzye_592x888.jpg)

Who says you can't be casual AND classy at the same time? Serena always knew how to perfect this look. Channel her style and pair some skinny jeans with a silk blouse (tucked in, of course). Throw on a patterned blazer over it to give your outfit a sophisticated vibe. Don't forget the knee-high boots - Serena's looks aren't complete without them!

Well, there you have it. These are just seven out of many awesome ways to channel Gossip Girl in your wardrobe this fall. Even though the show's been off the air for quite some time now, the style they've shown is classic and timeless. It's very inspirational when it comes to fall fashion! What's your favorite way to channel your inner Gossip Girl?

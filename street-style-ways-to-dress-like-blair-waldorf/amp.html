7 Street Style Ways to Dress like Blair Waldorf ...

Color Coordinate • Keep It Sleek and Chic • Professional and Sophisticated • Dare to Mix &amp; Match • Always Say Yes to Floral • More ...

By Vanessa

There are so many fun and easy ways to dress like Blair Waldorf. In Gossip Girl, Blair pulls off some outfits that, let's face it, only Blair can pull off. However, that doesn't mean we can't rock a similar style. It's all about being confident in your own skin and being comfortable enough to take fashion risks, no matter what anyone else thinks. 

There are style icons, and then there is Blair Waldorf. The Queen Bee of the Upper East Side, Blair’s wardrobe on Gossip Girl wasn’t just fashionable - it was a character all its own, dripping with elegance, power, and effortless sophistication. Years after the show’s original run, her influence endures, inspiring a new generation of fashion lovers who dream of bringing a little bit of that Waldorf magic into their everyday lives. Whether she was striding through the marble halls of Constance Billard in a perfectly tailored coat and headband, or making a grand entrance at a society gala in layers of tulle and pearls, Blair’s outfits always told a story: one of ambition, romance, wit, and unapologetic femininity.

But what makes Blair’s style truly timeless isn’t just the designer labels or the luxe fabrics - it’s the careful balance between classic refinement and playful experimentation. She mastered the art of layering textures, mixing bold prints, and incorporating statement accessories in ways that never felt overdone. And while her wardrobe was undeniably aspirational, it was also surprisingly adaptable, offering endless inspiration for real-world, street-style looks.

Today, channeling Blair Waldorf isn’t about replicating her outfits piece-for-piece - it’s about capturing her spirit: the confidence to wear what you love, the fearlessness to make a statement, and the elegance to do it all with grace. Whether you’re brunching with friends, heading to a chic event, or simply want to turn the sidewalk into your personal runway, there are countless ways to weave Blair’s iconic style into your everyday wardrobe.

In this article, we’re unveiling 7 street style ways to dress like Blair Waldorf, bringing a modern twist to her most iconic looks. Get ready to embrace structured silhouettes, embrace ladylike accessories, and most importantly, unleash your inner Queen Bee.

1. Color Coordinate

Even though Blair always paired different patterns together, they always coordinated. That's all you need to remember when accessorizing an outfit. For example, if you have a splash of the color red in your outfit, you can coordinate it with a matching red lip! This is just one of the ways to dress like Blair Waldorf.

2. Keep It Sleek and Chic

When dressing like Blair Waldorf, always keep things chic. For me, that means rocking an all black ensemble. I think it's sleek, sexy, and sophisticated. You can "Blair" the outfit up by adding a beautiful silk scarf, one of her signature berets, or some colorful heels.

3. Professional and Sophisticated

One thing's for sure - Blair always looks like she's ready to take over the world. She always keeps it stylish, professional, and totally sophisticated. To recreate this look, pair a blouse with a black pencil skirt; you can add some color with accessories like a headband, purse, scarf, etc. You'll look very office-ready!

4. Dare to Mix & Match

What's the fun in always matching? Blair's never afraid to experiment with different colors and patterns. I've seen so many of her outfits where I think, "Wow, I wouldn't have thought of pairing those two items together but they look awesome!" It's all about the confidence that you carry. If you think you look good, the whole world will think you look good.

5. Always Say Yes to Floral

You can never go wrong with floral and Blair definitely knows that. It looks flattering on everyone and it's a huge trend for the summer. I love the idea of mixing & matching two separate floral pieces - as long as the colors coordinate, you'll look amazing! If you think that's too "overwhelming," try an outfit like a floral top with a black skirt.

6. Be Daring with Color

One of my favorite things about Blair's style is the fact that she's so daring. I'm sure you've seen her signature red tights (who knew they could be so cute?!) and the awesome prints she's worn.  She's not afraid to make a fashion statement-and she's definitely not afraid of having heads turn as she walks on by. If you’re looking to channel that same bold energy, try stepping out in funky colored compression socks.

7. Shop Till You Drop

One of the most important things to remember when trying to dress like Blair Waldorf is that you HAVE to keep changing things up. That means you have to constantly switch up your wardrobe and bring in all your new goodies. Of course, not all of us can afford to shop like Queen B does - if that's the case, mix and match some items that you already have and reinvent your style that way!

Well, there you have it. These are just seven out of many awesome street style ways to dress like Blair Waldorf. Even though Gossip Girl is no longer on TV, Blair's still a fashion inspiration to so many girls. She always carried herself with such confidence and always looked fabulous no matter what! How do you like to channel your inner Blair Waldorf?

Frequently Asked Questions

What are some signature elements of Blair Waldorf's style?
Blair's style is all about classic, preppy elegance! Think headbands, tailored coats, pleated skirts, and chic dresses. She loves rich fabrics like tweed and silk and often sports bows, lace, and tights. Of course, accessories are key, especially her signature headbands!
How can I incorporate Blair Waldorf's look into my everyday street style?
You can start by adding a few signature pieces like a structured blazer or a cute pleated skirt to your wardrobe. Don't forget a stylish headband or a statement necklace. Mixing and matching these with your everyday basics can go a long way!
What modern brands should I check out to find Blair-inspired pieces?
Brands like Kate Spade, J.Crew, and Tory Burch offer some fabulous pieces that channel Blair's preppy-chic vibes. For a more affordable option, check out Zara or H&M as they often have great blazers, skirts, and accessories.
Do I need to stick to a specific color palette to nail Blair Waldorf's style?
Blair often wears a lot of classic colors like navy, white, black, and red, but she also loves rich jewel tones like emerald green and deep purples. You don't have to stick strictly to these colors, but opting for a more classic and cohesive palette can help capture her look.
Can I still achieve Blair Waldorf's style on a budget?
Absolutely! Thrift stores and vintage shops can be goldmines for Blair-inspired pieces. Look for structured blazers, collared blouses, and unique accessories. You don’t need to break the bank; it’s all about finding those key pieces that add that Blair magic to your ensemble.

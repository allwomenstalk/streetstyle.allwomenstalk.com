# 7 Street Style Ways to Dress up Your Denim ...

There are so many awesome and easy ways to dress up your denim. Jeans are no longer just for casual outfits. You can easily make them look super high-fashion just by adding some accessories into the mix. I think every girl should have a stack of denim in her closet - whether it be jeans, a shirt, a jacket, whatever! Here's some cool ways to dress up your denim:

## 1. Tall Boots

![Tall Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nl/ct/e3ut71z7_433x650.jpg)

Knee-high boots are perfect for the fall. It's such a great transitional piece! Since they're already a statement piece, try and keep the rest of the outfit simple. You can either wear some dark wash jeans with your boots or skip the jeans and add a denim jacket to the outfit instead. Either way, the boots will make your outfit super stylish - try [macys.com](http://www1.macys.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)! This is just one of many cool ways to dress up your denim.

## 2. Statement Heels

![Statement Heels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yp/tb/p9nzsvmc_600x900.jpg)

Statement heels are constantly proving that shoes can really take an outfit to the next level. If you're wearing them, be sure to roll up your jeans so that the world can easily admire your eye-catching heels! You can opt for a bold color (two-toned heels are my favorite) or go for a fun animal-print! I would definitely go for some cheetah print. It's fun, youthful, and of course, super sexy! What kind of print is your favorite?

## 3. Wear a Lace Top

![Wear a Lace Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mj/hn/uykezvtc_600x1067.jpg)

I think lace is a perfect balance between sexy and sophisticated, which means it's a must-have piece for every girl's closet! You can wear a lace top with some skinnies or even some distressed boyfriend jeans! I think a tailored top looks great with boyfriend jeans because it dresses up the denim to look a bit more chic! Don't forget to add some accessories - keep it simple though, the lace top is already intricate enough!

## 4. Blazer

![Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nk/hd/v1n7elvt_420x630.jpg)

Blazers can make anything look classy and turn any outfit into an office-ready look. For a business casual look, you can pair your jeans with a blouse and a blazer. It's amazing how a simple blazer can instantly dress up your denim, huh?

## 5. Maxi Skirt

![Maxi Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jx/ax/os8ma8og_599x898.jpg)

Who said maxi skirts are solely for the summer? You can totally wear this trend in the fall as a transitional piece, so don't get rid of them just yet! Throw on a denim jacket over your maxi skirt and it'll give you this 'effortlessly cool' and bohemian type of vibe.

## 6. Colorblock

![Colorblock](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bx/ck/tkzx145e_600x927.jpg)

Color blocking is a cool and easy way to add, well, color to your outfit. You can add a color blocked sweater or jacket over your outfit and it'll definitely add some "sophisticated style" points. I love this color blocked blazer from [forever21.com](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)! They have a huge selection of cute (and affordable) color block options to pair with your denim!

## 7. Accessorize

![Accessorize](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yr/lh/jn2jpsdp_600x899.jpg)

An easy way to take any outfit to the next level is by adding tons of cool accessories! This could mean chunky necklaces, rings, or even a nice purse. I mean, we all know that accessories could make or break an outfit, right?

Well, there you have it. These are just seven out of many awesome ways that you can dress up your denim! Of the list, which is your favorite? How do YOU like to dress up your denim?

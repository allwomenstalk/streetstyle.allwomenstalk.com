# 7 Street Style Ways to Look Patriotic This Summer ...

There are so many cool ways to look patriotic this summer. The Fourth of July is finally here and these are all outfits that you can easily recreate. It's really amazing how many different ways the American flag design can be incorporated into your outfit. Whether it be on your jacket, shoes, or pants, the American flag design will never go out of style. Check out some of the cool street style ways to look patriotic this summer and then let me know which looks are your favorites!

## 1. Blouse + Skirt

_ If you don't want to wear the actual American flag design, there are ways you can still look patriotic and be subtle about it. The American flag features stripes and stars, so that's all you need. Wear a star-patterned blouse (you can find some at [Nordstrom](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)) and pair it with a striped maxi skirt. You can find a red/white striped skirt at Forever 21_. This is just one of many cool ways to look patriotic this summer. P.S. you can totally switch up the stripes and stars!

## 2. Jacket

![Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/py/c5/svlr2gtz_600x900.jpg)

You can find a bunch of awesome and expressive jackets that feature the American flag design on [Etsy](https://www.etsy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com). Prices vary based on design but I'm sure you'll find one that you love! It's a nice to have and just throw over your outfit. It's festive and patriotic- what more could you want?!

## 3. Shorts

_ Since summertime is all about denim cutoffs, why not add some style points by wearing the ones with the American flag? They're a cute way to show off your stripes and stars! Pair them with white sandals and a white top for a finished look. If you like the idea of a 'faded' flag, same design/no color, check out the shorts from Forever 21_.

## 4. Scarf

_ Scarves are always a great accessory. I really like the old-fashioned look and beltoutlet.com_ sells a great American flag scarf! Of course, you can also look at stores like Target to find modern, affordable ones.

## 5. Bikini

![Bikini](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b0/cs/ykynw61e_420x600.jpg)

An awesome way to show your patriotism is to rock the American flag bikini! You can wear the stripes and stars at pool parties or bonfires at the beach. Stores like [American Apparel](https://goo.gl/X4Y6tT) sell some really cool ones, so I definitely suggest checking them out!

## 6. Bandana

![Bandana](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/es/qb/pwewr726_600x900.jpg)

Bandanas are becoming the must-have item this summer! It's a cool way to add some pizzazz to your outfit. There are tons of cool patterns that you can find, and of course, the American flag one is adorable! You can find some on [Etsy](https://www.etsy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com). They're all very affordable and super stylish!

## 7. Leggings

![Leggings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c2/tp/hcer68n7_600x900.jpg)

American flag leggings are awesome! You can find some at [American Apparel](https://goo.gl/d2oLiU) that'll be perfect for your Fourth of July BBQ! It's definitely a statement piece and will no doubt be the focal point of your outfit. So, keep the accessories to a minimum. I'd suggest pairing the leggings with a basic white tee. What do you think of these leggings?

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of many awesome street style ways to look patriotic this summer. Whether it be on the Fourth of July or just a regular day, these are some fun and easy ways to wear the American flag! Of the list, which look is your favorite? Which will you be recreating?

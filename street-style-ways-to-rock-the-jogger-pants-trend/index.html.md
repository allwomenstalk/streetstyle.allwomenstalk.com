# 7 Street Style Ways to Rock the Jogger Pants Trend ...

There are so many awesome ways that you can rock the jogger pants trend this spring. It's definitely in style, I've seen them everywhere! I think it's such a cool look and the pants kind of add this instant "edgy" vibe to your outfit. You can dress the pants up or down - depending on how you want your outfit to look. Check out some of the cool ways you can rock the jogger pants trend. If you have any other ideas/tips, share them in the comments section.

## 1. Leather

![Leather](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b7/a1/o5wh3rgq_600x900.jpg)

You can never go wrong with leather, right? I think it can add an instant cool and edgy vibe to any outfit. Stars like Selena Gomez have rocked the leather jogger pants and you can too. Pair your leather joggers with a graphic tee or a denim button up. Denim and leather look great together. You can find leather joggers on [karmaloop.com](http://www.karmaloop.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com). This is just one of the ways that you can rock the jogger pants trend.

## 2. Silk

![Silk](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yf/ce/ab6t4pg2_492x736.jpg)

I love this! It's such a girly and feminine way to rock this trend. You can find some silk jogger pants on [armaniexchange.com](https://goo.gl/7xqFSM). Silk jogger pants allow you to dress up the outfit, making it look sophisticated and classy. Are you a fan of this look?

## 3. Prints

![Prints](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/da/yq/lny6j9ve_500x733.jpg)

Prints are always so much fun to rock. It's an easy way to make a fashion statement. Pair your printed jogger pants with a plain tee, so it's not too overwhelming. I love the leather jacket in this look. Major style points, right? You can find printed jogger pants on [asos.com](https://goo.gl/PVxGnz).

## 4. Black + Gold

_ I absolutely the combination of black and gold. I think it looks great together. You can find sparkly gold joggers on glamourholiconlineshop.com_ (along with so many other fantastic items). I think this look is very street-chic. I love the boots and the white collared shirt underneath the black sweater.

## 5. Denim

![Denim](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qo/xd/s3u6wbz4_600x900.jpg)

I'm obsessed with this look. Denim joggers look amazing paired with anything. I love the flowy printed tank in this look. It makes the outfit really girly and feminine. You can find denim joggers on [asos.com](https://goo.gl/mkbqPX). I love the light acid wash of the denim. It's very cute!

## 6. With a Crop Top

![With a Crop Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/up/bv/xd9hoys2_450x643.jpg)

One of the easiest ways to rock the jogger pants trend is to pair it with a crop top. You can opt for a fun printed shirt, or a classic black or white crop top. It'd look great with printed joggers or solid colors. What kind of crop top would you pair your jogger pants with?

## 7. With a Graphic Tee

![With a Graphic Tee](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s5/oi/nhkb0hc3_500x666.jpg)

I think graphic tees add an instant edgy vibe to any outfit. So I love the idea of having it paired with some jogger pants. I like that these joggers are a neutral grey color. It goes with the pop of red on the tee. Of course, no look is complete without some leather. I love the leather vest and black cross body bag. It totally completes the outfit, don't you think?

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of many awesome ways you can start to rock the jogger pants trend. Of the list, which look is your favorite? How do you like to wear your jogger pants? Share outfit ideas and/or tips in the comments section!

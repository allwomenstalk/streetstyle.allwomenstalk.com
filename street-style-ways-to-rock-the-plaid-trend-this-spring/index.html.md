# 7 Street Style Ways to Rock the Plaid Trend This Spring ...

There are so many awesome ways that you can rock the plaid trend this year. I'm sure you've noticed it pretty much everywhere. Everyone's going mad for plaid! Ha, see what I did there? Anyways, plaid is one of my favorite patterns. I just feel like it goes great with anything and everything. Check out some of the cool ways that you can rock the plaid trend this spring. If you have any other ideas/tips, share them in the comments section!

## 1. Romper

![Romper](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ns/d5/ahzh6rw1_600x900.jpg)

I may or may not be totally obsessed with this look! Yup, I am. It's such a cute take on a romper! I think the romper is already a statement piece, so if you want to recreate this look, keep the accessories to a minimum. You'll look quite the fashionista. You can find similar rompers on [shopbop.com](http://www.shopbop.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com). This is just one of the cute ways to rock the plaid trend this spring.

## 2. Jacket

![Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qx/ss/nq98zf4q_600x784.jpg)

I honestly believe that a plaid coat is a must-have in any girl's closet! It's become my go-to item because it goes so well with everything. Plaid's just one of those patterns that you can throw on top of anything. Do you own a plaid coat?

## 3. Suit

![Suit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ug/me/txymbjiq_600x900.jpg)

If you want to spice up your style in the workplace, try this plaid suit. I'm LOVING this look! It's such a fun and stylish way to add some pizazz to your outfit. Would you wear this to work? You can find similar ones at Kohls.

## 4. Pants

_ If you want to make a cool fashion statement, try plaid pants! They're bold and at the same time, very flattering.You can pair your plaid pants with a graphic tee to give it an "edgier" vibe. You can find cool plaid pants on forever21.com_. Would you rock plaid pants?

## 5. Skirt

![Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wt/0m/via6julc_600x900.jpg)

One of the most stylish ways to rock the plaid trend this spring is with a plaid skirt. I think they're so cute! You can find a bunch of different styles at Forever 21. I would pair my plaid skirt with a fun graphic tee - it gives the outfit an edgy vibe. For shoes, I'd go with some combat boots. How would you style your plaid skirt?

## 6. On Your Waist

![On Your Waist](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hk/g4/z89tcv9u_600x900.jpg)

One of the easiest ways to rock the plaid trend this spring is tie a plaid shirt around your waist. I've seen this look everywhere and I still think it's awesome. Rock a plain white with some black skinnies, or some denim cut offs. Then, just tie your plaid button up around your waist for an "effortlessly cool" look. Celebrities like Pretty Little Liars' Ashley Benson and Vanessa Hudgens are always rocking this look.

## 7. Dress

![Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eo/pn/wbt9d0m1_500x750.jpg)

I'm in love with this! Spring is all about dresses and right now, it's all about plaid. You can find similar dresses on [forever21.com](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) or [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com). They both offer stylish ways to rock the plaid trend. For an edgy vibe, throw a leather jacket over the dress. For shoes, combat boots would look awesome and complete the whole look.

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of many awesome ways that you can start rocking the plaid trend. Of the list, which is your favorite look? Are you going 'mad' for plaid? Share any outfit ideas/tips that you have in the comments section.

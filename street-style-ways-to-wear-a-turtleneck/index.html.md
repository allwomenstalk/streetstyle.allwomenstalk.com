# Street Style Ways to Wear a Turtleneck ...

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oi/m3/fvty3upn560305f10e57a103772516_276x276.jpg)

Turtlenecks cover up a lot of skin, but that doesn't mean they can't be sexy. Over at [In Style](https://www.instyle.com/fashion/how-style-wear-turtleneck-fall), they figured out a few cute ways to create fashionable outfits that involve turtlenecks. Don't believe it? Check it out right here:

## 1. With an EVENING SKIRT

![With an EVENING SKIRT](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cf/tm/blwtee6p560305fc5ab7f949433788_600x445.jpg)

You don't have to wear sweatpants with your turtleneck. You can keep things classy with an evening skirt.

## 2. With a PLUNGING NECKLINE

![With a PLUNGING NECKLINE](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q4/ea/bvltqa94560305f1bbbcc070013302_600x445.jpg)

Your turtleneck is going to cover up a lot of skin, but you can still wear a low-cut shirt over it.

## 3. With a POLO

![With a POLO](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ot/aq/pxmu0xqa560305fad4c46072963301_600x445.jpg)

If you're going to wear a turtleneck to work, you better pair it with a polo!

## 4. With OVERALLS

![With OVERALLS](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cf/ga/ehk4f8g3560305faf2987792753189_600x445.jpg)

You need to place something under your overalls, anyway, so you might as well pull on a turtleneck.

## 5. With a CUT-out DRESS

![With a CUT-out DRESS](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qf/ps/zv3lzsym560305fce2245269344543_600x445.jpg)

A cut-out dress is the key to looking sexy while wearing a turtleneck.

## 6. With a STRAPPY DRESS

![With a STRAPPY DRESS](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wq/yd/q7axy6nz560305fd98e40541524843_600x445.jpg)

Here's another dress you can wear with your finest turtleneck.

## 7. With SUIT SEPARATES

![With SUIT SEPARATES](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j2/tk/m1uaitei56030605b861d561577971_600x445.jpg)

If you're feeling chilly at work, you might as well wear a turtleneck beneath your suit.

See? You don't have to feel frumpy in a turtleneck. What do you usually wear when you slip one of them on?

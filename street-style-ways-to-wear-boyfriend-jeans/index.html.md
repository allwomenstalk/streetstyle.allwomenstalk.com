# 11 Street Style Ways to Wear Boyfriend Jeans ...

There are plenty of different ways to wear boyfriend jeans. Boyfriend jeans are a trend that seems to be going strong, and they’re a favourite with fashionistas the world over. You can dress them down for a streetwear-inspired look or dress them up for a more polished take on the trend. Take a look at these ways to wear boyfriend jeans, as seen on the streets.

## 1. Light Denim and Leather

![Light Denim and Leather](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/08/1_light-denim-and-leather_432x648.jpg)

When it comes to ways to wear boyfriend jeans, many outfits revolve around streetwear-inspired looks. Dani was snapped on the streets on New York City by Anton Lombardi wearing faded denim boyfriend jeans with a slouchy American Apparel top and Topshop leather-look biker jacket. Oversized sunglasses and Converse sneakers completed this casual daytime look.

## 2. Denim on Denim

![Denim on Denim](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/08/2_denim-on-denim_600x1025.jpg)

Try your hand at denim on denim, like this outfit we spotted on the Stylesight blog. Team your boyfriend jeans with a button down denim shirt, and accessorise with ladylike essentials like a statement necklace, pointed pumps, and quilted handbag. A tailored coat worn over the shoulders completes this fashion-forward look.

## 3. Jeans and a Tee

![Jeans and a Tee](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/08/3_jeans-and-a-tee_600x900.jpg)

Denim jeans and white t-shirts are a classic combination. Adam Katz Sinding snapped this casual take on the classic outfit combination. Team distressed denim jeans, cuffed at the ankles, with a slouchy white t-shirt, flat sandals, and a small side bag for a casual summer look.

## 4. Denim and Fur

![Denim and Fur](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/08/4_denim-and-fur_500x750.jpg)

Dress your boyfriend jeans up for fall with a furry jacket or coat. We spotted this look on the Topshop Tumblr and think it’s one you could easily recreate. Team your boyfriend jeans with a timeless striped tee, shaggy fur (or faux fur) coat, and knitted beanie for a cozy cold weather look. Here, brogues give the outfit menswear-inspired feel but you could swap them for some ladylike pumps or ballet flats.

## 5. Mint Denim

![Mint Denim](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/08/5_mint-denim_432x626.jpg)

Coloured denim is still enjoying its time in the limelight. Maria was photographed by Anton Lombardi working a cool and casual look in mint coloured jeans and a slouchy white tank top. Complete with tan shoes and a handbag, this is a fresh outfit for spring and summer.

## 6. Grunge Inspired

![Grunge Inspired](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/08/6_grunge-inspired_592x888.jpg)

Nineties-inspired fashion is enjoying a comeback of sorts, so give your boyfriend jeans the grunge treatment. Charlotte was snapped by Suzanne Middlemass wearing vintage jeans, a Topshop biker jacket, and brown Dr Martens shoes. A black ribbed beanie completes this grunge-inspired look.

## 7. Pretty in Pink

![Pretty in Pink](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/08/7_pretty-in-pink_600x900.jpg)

Blogger Audrey, of Sushi Pedro, teams her slouchy boyfriend jeans with pink accents. She pairs her jeans with a white top, pink flecked cardigan, and a hot pink side bag. Black sunglasses and tan loafers complete this casual daytime look.

## 8. Denim and Florals

![Denim and Florals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/08/8_denim-and-florals_600x906.jpg)

We spotted this floral look on blogger Valentine from Hello It’s Valentine. She works an eclectic look by teaming her boyfriend jeans with a button down floral shirt, polka dot platform heels, and a hot pink side bag.

## 9. Polka Dot Prints

![Polka Dot Prints](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/08/9_polka-dot-prints_392x560.jpg)

Blogger Jules, from Sincerely Jules, teams her mid-wash boyfriend jeans with a sleeveless polka dot blouse for a smart casual daytime look. She also sports some aviator sunglasses, an oversized clutch, and neutral strappy heels. This is a stylish outfit that would surely stand the test of time.

## 10. Workwear Inspired

![Workwear Inspired](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/08/10_workwear-inspired_600x900.jpg)

Team your favourite boyfriend jeans with a button down shirt and blazer for a workwear-inspired look. This outfit was snapped by Adam Katz Sinding and looks right at home on the city streets. Recreate this look by teaming a crisp collared shirt, fitted blazer, and pumps with your favourite boyfriend jeans.

## 11. Distressed Denim

![Distressed Denim](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/08/11_distressed-denim_600x867.jpg)

Dress up some severely ripped jeans with classic, polished pieces. Sarah from A House in The Hills teams her distressed denim boyfriend jeans with a classic striped top and skinny strap heels. A leopard print clutch, wide cuff, and bright lipstick add fun touches to the entire outfit.

These are just some examples of how to style boyfriend jeans. Are you a fan of the boyfriend jean? Which of these street style looks do you like best?

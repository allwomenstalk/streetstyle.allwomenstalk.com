# 7 Street Style Ways to Wear Leather Pants during the Day ...

There are a number of stylish ways to wear leather pants during the day. Leather and leather-look pants have been gaining popularity for some time now. The trend is usually associated with dressier and more evening-appropriate outfits but you can make them work during the day. They’re great for working sporty and smart casual outfits. Get your leather on and take a look at these ways to wear leather pants during the day.

## 1. Tough Love

![Tough Love](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/03210042_4728_600x900.jpg)

When it comes to ways to wear leather pants during the day, Kathi from The Daily Dose goes for a tougher look when styling her leather pants. She teams her skinny leather pants with a cosy grey sweater and lace up ankle boots. The soft knitwear nicely balances out the harder edge of the leather and lace up boots. A slouchy chain trim handbag completes this edgy urban look.

## 2. Sporty Accents

![Sporty Accents](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/03210042_4099_600x908.jpg)

Take a sporty approach to dressing this winter and team your leather pants with a pair of statement sneakers. Raydene Salina snapped this outfit for The Huffington Post and it’s a cosy sport luxe look. The combination of leather, stripes and camel coat is classic but the colourful beanie and hi-top sneakers add some sporty flair.

## 3. In Colour

![In Colour](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/03210042_6211_600x900.jpg)

Black leather and leather-look pants are everywhere. Why not stand out from the crowd in some coloured leather pants? This is one statement making look we spotted on FabSugar. The bright red leather is nicely balanced out by a grey t-shirt and black blazer. The loafers are also a cool daytime touch.

## 4. Tailored

![Tailored](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/03210044_3917_600x941.jpg)

If your style is more polished and tailored, try a pair of leather pants in a tailored cut. We spotted this outfit on the Stylesight blog and think it’s a great example of how to dress up your daytime leather. The outfit of leather pants teamed with a plain white t-shirt and strappy heels is simple and understated. Clubmaster sunglasses, side bag, and cream knit sweater worn around the shoulders complete this cool daytime look.

## 5. With Denim

![With Denim](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/03210042_7878_600x900.jpg)

Dress down your leather pants with a cool denim shirt or jacket. Sheryl was featured in Lucky Mag wearing her leather pants with an oversized denim shirt. She teamed it with a cool triangle clutch, chrome oxford shoes, and aviator sunglasses. This is a relaxed everyday look that still manages to pack a punch.

## 6. Edgy Accessories

![Edgy Accessories](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/03210042_1239_592x888.jpg)

Ferne was photographed by Suzanne Middlemass wearing her skinny leather pants with a mix of classic and trend-based accessories. The combination of black leather pants and camel coloured coat is quite classic. The chunky cut out boots add a grunge feel while the fringed handbag and floppy felt hat give off a bohemian vibe.

## 7. Floral Accents

![Floral Accents](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/03210044_4991_600x959.jpg)

Add a feminine touch to your leather look with floral accents. Maegan from Love Maegan wears her leather-look pants with a floral sweatshirt. Even though she wears her outfit with pointed pumps, some ballet flats or sneakers would also make this a great daytime look.

Whether you prefer pretty polished looks or something with a bit more urban flair, there are a number of ways to style a pair of leather pants during the day. These are just some outfit examples, as seen on the streets. Do you have any tips for styling leather pants during the day?

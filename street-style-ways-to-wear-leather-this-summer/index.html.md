# 7 Street Style Ways to Wear Leather This Summer ...

There are so many street style ways to wear leather and look fashionable this summer. It's definitely a trend that'll stay in style for a long time to come. That's why it's a good idea to rock it this summer! There's so many different ways that you can wear it and dress it up or down. Check out some of the cool street style ways to wear leather this summer. Feel free to share your outfit ideas/tips in the comments section!

## 1. Leather Vest

_ A leather vest is a great addition to any summer outfit. It instantly adds an 'edgy' vibe to your outfit and gives you that effortlessly cool look. You can find some awesome leather vests on forever21.com_. This is just one of many street style ways to wear leather this summer!

## 2. Pants

![Pants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dl/j1/cunr51hd_600x858.jpg)

An easy way to wear leather this summer is with some leather leggings/pants. You can find some on [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)! You can style your outfit with a graphic tee, crop top, or loose-fitting shirt. For shoes, I'd go with some combat boots! If you feel like dressing it up, swap it for some pumps.

## 3. Leather on Leather

![Leather on Leather](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j5/up/f4frrzuv_400x600.jpg)

Leather on leather may seem a bit overwhelming, but it always looks good! Don't be afraid to throw a leather jacket over your leather leggings. If you want to go major rebel-chic, go with an all-black ensemble. It's sleek, sexy, and sophisticated! What do you think of the leather on leather look?

## 4. High-waisted Shorts

![High-waisted Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/0b/of/s12q4k5h_600x896.jpg)

High-waisted leather shorts are a cool addition to any girly outfit. You can find some shorts on [us.riverisland.com](http://us.riverisland.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com). To recreate this look, throw a blazer over your outfit. I'd go with a patterned one just to add some pizazz!

## 5. Jacket

![Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k6/vt/hgsz00yq_600x983.jpg)

A leather jacket is a must-have in every girl's closet. It's the perfect thing to throw over your summer outfit. You can pull off the rocker-chic look this summer with some combat boots, denim cut offs, and a graphic tee. The leather jacket pulls your outfit together and completes the whole look. It's my go-to leather piece in my closet!

## 6. Peplum Top

![Peplum Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qj/n2/nkso6cr1_600x899.jpg)

Peplum tops are great for the summer time! It's a light and breezy option for summer attire. You can pair a peplum top with shorts or skinny jeans! Your shoes can either dress the outfit up or down, making it more casual or formal. For this outfit, I'd go with flats. I love the casual look - comfortable, yet stylish! If you want a formal look, swap the flats and take your outfit to the next level by wearing some high heels.

## 7. Dress

![Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yo/cf/ehxlu7v6_600x901.jpg)

What I love most about leather dresses is the many different ways you can accessorize them. Since the summer time is all about bright and neon colors, try incorporating them through your jewelry. A statement necklace will earn you major style points. You could add a splash of color to your outfit with a neon cross body bag. You can find some cool dresses on [nastygal.com](http://www.nastygal.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com). How would you accessorize your leather dress?

Well, there you have it. These are just seven out of many awesome street style ways to wear leather this summer. Of the list, which look is your favorite? Which look will you be recreating this summer?

# 9 Street Style Ways to Wear Leopard Print like a Wild Cat ...

There are plenty of ways to wear leopard print clothes. Leopard print is a pretty timeless trend, and this season there’s a leopard print piece of clothing to suit most tastes. From classic coats to on-trend statement pants, you’re spoilt for choice when it comes to this wild animal print. Check out these trendy ways to wear leopard print.

## 1. Leopard Print Coat

![Leopard Print Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/08/1_leopard-print-coat_600x900.jpg)

One of the most popular ways to wear leopard print is in the form of a coat. The leopard print coat looks super stylish worn with an all-black outfit, like this look we spotted via Stockholm Street Style. The leopard print Zara coat is a great addition to the black leather-look pants, sweater, and oversized square sunglasses.

## 2. Leopard Print Top

![Leopard Print Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/08/2_leopard-print-top_432x596.jpg)

This cool street style look was snapped by Anton Lombardi for Elle magazine. Here, Caitlin teams her vintage leopard print top with Levi’s denim shorts and short flat ankle boots. A statement necklace, large tote bag, and oversized sunglasses complete her look. This leopard print outfit looks right at home in this urban jungle.

## 3. Leopard Print Skirt

![Leopard Print Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/08/3_leopard-print-skirt_600x963.jpg)

Work a ladylike look with a twist in a leopard print pencil skirt. We spotted this lady on the Stylesight blog wearing her leopard print skirt with a studded black sweater and neutral pointed pumps. The look is a mix of feminine and edgy styling and is a simple yet striking way to work the trend into your everyday wardrobe.

## 4. Leopard Print Pants

![Leopard Print Pants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/08/4_leopard-print-pants_600x900.jpg)

Printed pants are an easy way to add a little bit of wow factor to your outfit. Try some leopard print pants for a cool everyday look. This stunning lady was photographed by Street Style Seconds. She wears her drawstring leopard print pants with a mustard coloured shirt and block heeled sandals. This is a smart daytime look but you could also dress it down with a slouchy singlet and floppy wide-brimmed hat.

## 5. Leopard and Leather

![Leopard and Leather](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/08/5_leopard-and-leather_600x937.jpg)

Warm up your fall/winter leather looks with a modern leopard print jacket. We spotted this look on the Stylesight blog and think it’s the perfect blend of classic and modern tailoring. The structured coat, pointed pumps, and classic handbag balance out against the slouchy leather jogger-style pants.

## 6. Leopard Print Jeans

![Leopard Print Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/08/6_leopard-print-jeans_333x500.jpg)

Here, Angela was snapped by the Refinery 29 photographers in this street smart get-up. It’s a unique take on denim on denim, as she’s teamed her Current/Elliot leopard print jeans with a classic denim shirt. A layered necklace and black ankle boots complete this casually cool outfit.

## 7. Colourful Leopard Print

![Colourful Leopard Print](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/08/7_colourful-leopard-print_500x750.jpg)

If traditional leopard print bores you, why not try a colourful version instead? Lately we’ve been seeing leopard prints that cover a whole spectrum of colours. We spotted this colourful leopard print look on the Topshop Tumblr and think it’s a great way to up the ante when it comes to leopard print. Teamed with a leather jacket and super cool print sweater, this outfit just exudes attitude.

## 8. Leopard on Leopard

![Leopard on Leopard](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/08/8_leopard-on-leopard_500x750.jpg)

While head-to-toe leopard print might have been a faux pas in the past, we’ve actually spotted a more than a few trendsetters rocking the leopard on leopard look. Eleanora Carisi was snapped by the Topshop street style photographers wearing this triple leopard look. Want to know the key to making leopard on leopard work? Just own it!

## 9. Leopard Print Accents

![Leopard Print Accents](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/08/9_leopard-print-accents_550x825.jpg)

If leopard print clothing is too much, try incorporating some leopard print accents and accessories into your outfit instead. It could be a leopard scarf, shoes, or, like this look we spotted on STREETFSN by Nam, a compact leopard print clutch.

Whether you wear your leopard print as an accent or a neutral, there are plenty of different ways to make this wild print work for you. Are you a fan of leopard print? Which of these outfits do you like best?

# Street Style Ways to Wear off the Shoulder Shirts for Spring ...

You don't have to slip into a tank-top or a sleeveless dress to show off your shoulders. Since it's still a bit chilly out, you should think about wearing off the shoulder shirts. That way, your shoulders will be exposed, but the rest of your arm can stay warm. If you own a few of these hot fashion items, here are some cute ways to wear off the shoulder shirts during Spring:

## 1. Wear It without a Bra

When you wear off the shoulder shirts, your shoulders will be exposed. That means that everyone will be able to see your bra straps. There's nothing wrong with that, because everyone knows that you're wearing a bra anyway, but it can ruin your look. If you want your shoulders to be completely bare, then think about wearing a strapless bra. If your shirt is thick enough, then you don't even have to wear a bra at all. There's nothing wrong with going natural!

## 2. Wear It with a Scarf

If it's still pretty cold out, you can wear a scarf with your off the shoulder shirt. That way, you'll cover up some of your exposed skin, but you'll still feel fashionable. After all, the more accessories you use, the better your outfit will end up looking.

## 3. Wear It with Leggings

Everyone tells you not to wear leggings as pants, but there's no rule against it--especially if your shirt goes down past your booty. If it covers up the area, then you should feel free to wear leggings with your off the shoulder shirt. It'll look adorable.

## 4. Wear It with a Bralette

Off the shoulder shirts are meant to be worn in warm weather, which is why some of them are so thin. If your shirt is see-through, then you should think about swapping out your regular bra for a bralette. That way, you won't mind if people get a peek of what's underneath your shirt, because it'll look more like a fashion choice than an accident.

## 5. Wear It with a Fashionable Pair of Jeans

Off the shoulder shirts are trendy, but they don't have to be fancy. If you want to wear them with jeans, then go ahead. It'll give you a casual look, and nothing's cuter than seeming like you've just rolled out of bed and automatically looked fabulous.

## 6. Wear It as a Dress

If your off the shoulder shirt is long enough to act as a dress, then you don't have to wear anything at all underneath it. You can use it as an opportunity to show off your sexy legs. That's what nice weather is for!

## 7. Wear It with Shorts

If the weather is nice enough, you can always wear your off the shoulder shirts with shorts. That way, you'll expose a decent chunk of skin, but it won't be anything too crazy. It's the perfect outfit to wear when you're going out on a date with your crush, because it'll strike the perfect balance between sexy and casual.

Now you have a few new ways to style your favorite top throughout the season! Do you own any off the shoulder shirts or are you going to have to visit the store soon to buy some?

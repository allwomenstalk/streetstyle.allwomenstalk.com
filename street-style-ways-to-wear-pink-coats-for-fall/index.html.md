# 9 Street Style Ways to Wear Pink Coats for Fall ...

Get on top of one of the biggest trends this season by taking a look at different ways to wear pink coats. Thanks to designers like Jil Sander and Simone Rocha, the pink coat is a must have item of clothing for fall. The pink coat is fun, flirty, and feminine. The rosy hue is also a welcome change from your usual fall colours of browns and burnt oranges. Be inspired by these ways to wear pink coats, as seen on the streets.

## 1. Contemporary Pink Coat

![Contemporary Pink Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/20201451_0363_500x750.jpg)

When it comes to ways to wear pink coats, make like Camille here and keep it contemporary. We spotted this stylish snap on the Topshop Tumblr. Camille wears a pink wool coat over a grey sweater and quilted mini skit. A slim clutch, chunky sandals, and oversized sunglasses complete this chic statement look.

## 2. Polka Dot Pink Coat

![Polka Dot Pink Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/20201451_7045_426x639.jpg)

Pink is quite a feminine colour, so you could play it up with extreme ladylike styling. Ploy was snapped by Dvora for Vogue wearing a polka dot pink coat. It’s a quirky feminine look which she has played up with glitter booties and large dangly earrings.

## 3. Candy Pink Coat

![Candy Pink Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/20201452_8757_600x986.jpg)

A pink coat is a stylish way to add a feminine touch to an otherwise masculine outfit. Jessie was spotted on the Stylesight blog wearing a candy pink coat with a mainly black and white outfit. The white t-shirt, cuffed trousers, and chunky loafers are quite masculine but the pink coat balances it all out quite nicely.

## 4. Cinched Pink Coat

![Cinched Pink Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/20201451_7251_600x900.jpg)

Irina was photographed by Amar Daved for Elle magazine wearing her pink coat cinched in at the waist. This is a great way of giving an oversized coat some shape. She wears her coat with blue denim jeans, a denim shirt, printed sandals, and a structured side bag. Leather cuffs peeping out from the sleeves also add an extra edge to the outfit.

## 5. Pastel Pink Coat

![Pastel Pink Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/20201452_3082_600x897.jpg)

Blogger Victoria Tornegren wears her pastel pink coat over a mostly sporty look. Underneath her coat she wears a white t-shirt, tight skinny jeans, and Converse sneakers. She accessorises simply with a clutch bag and dark sunglasses. This is one example of how a statement coat can update the most basic of outfits.

## 6. Furry Pink Coat

![Furry Pink Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/20201452_6787_600x900.jpg)

Blogger Mija teams a furry pastel pink coat with a mostly all black outfit. Her Topshop coat tops off an outfit of a lacy camisole, jogger-style trousers, basic black bag, and leopard print slip on shoes. It’s a mostly minimalist outfit with statement touches.

## 7. Sporty Pink Coat

![Sporty Pink Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/20201452_9798_500x750.jpg)

The pink coat doesn’t always have to be tailored or structured. Julia Sarr-Jamois works a casual street look in a hot pink trench coat and ripped boyfriend jeans. Teamed with reflective sunglasses, a printed top, and clashing red heels, this is a funky look that’s bound to get you noticed.

## 8. Oversized Pink Coat

![Oversized Pink Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/20201453_1526_427x640.jpg)

A thick oversized coat is bound to keep you warm when the temperature drops. Blogger Olivia wears her oversized pink coat with a speckled knit turtleneck, white skirt, and short boots. An oversized clutch complements the oversized coat as well.

## 9. Hot Pink Coat

![Hot Pink Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/20201454_9605_600x914.jpg)

Paulienne wears her hot pink coat with a simple outfit of a white blouse and skinny jeans. Slip on espadrille shoes and an oversized handbag complete this smart everyday look. This is the kind of look that you could easily recreate with your own wardrobe.

If street style trends are anything to go by, then the pink coat is definitely one to take notice of this season. Which of these street style looks do you like best? How would you style a pink coat for fall?

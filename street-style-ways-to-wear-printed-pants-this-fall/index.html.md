# 7 Street Style Ways to Wear Printed Pants This Fall ...

There are so many awesome and creative ways to wear printed pants this fall. Honestly, there's only one rule when it comes to wearing printed pants: tuck in your blouse and have fun with your accessories! You'll never have a dull outfit again because printed pants will instantly add an 'effortlessly cool' vibe to your outfit. Here are some fun ways to wear printed pants this fall:

## 1. Black and White

![Black and White](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/of/gt/wdap0lv2_600x900.jpg)

You can never go wrong with a black and white outfit because those two colors are timeless. You can choose between cool patterns or opt for a classic 'striped' look, like these pants from [Guess Factory](http://www.guessfactory.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com). If you want to dress up the look, throw on a blazer! This is just one of many ways to wear printed pants this fall.

## 2. High-waisted & Flared

![High-waisted & Flared](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qq/su/ys371v0f_600x914.jpg)

Who said the high-waisted trend is just for summer? You can totally wear this trend in the fall! To recreate this look, head on over to [Forever 21](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) for all your wide-leg needs. They have a huge selection of cute (and affordable) printed pants.

## 3. Plaid

![Plaid](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r3/v9/wwywfdqy_600x900.jpg)

Mad for plaid? Try the Tommy Hilfiger plaid cropped pant from [Macy's](http://www1.macys.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) for a laid-back feel. You can make your outfit a bit more preppy by layering a Peter Pan collar under a sweatshirt or cable knit sweater! Plaid pants are an awesome way to make a fashion statement.

## 4. Harem Pants

![Harem Pants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tx/su/qjgt2hwy_600x801.jpg)

I absolutely love harem pants... I just don't think I can pull them off, LOL! However, they're an awesome way to add some color, pattern, and excitement to your outfit. I really love the 'Starry Night' harem pants from [Free People](http://www.freepeople.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)! You can pair those types of pants with a solid colored tee, some boots, and if you want to add some edginess to the look, throw on a leather jacket.

## 5. Floral

![Floral](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jh/ll/d0xhjmbq_600x900.jpg)

I love all things floral. You just can't go wrong with it! I love the 'Floral Charlie Trousers' from [Anthropologie](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) - they look great with a loose-fitting blouse and some nice flats! If you're going for a more relaxed look, you can just opt for some floral leggings from [Forever 21](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com). Floral pants are a great statement piece to add to your outfit!

## 6. Animal Print

![Animal Print](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hl/p2/qvppinrh_500x733.jpg)

Wearing animal print is a trend that doesn't seem like it'll be going anywhere anytime soon. So, it's time to embrace it! There are so many cool looks that you can achieve with animal print. Try [Neiman Marcus](http://www.neimanmarcus.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) for your trouser needs - you can pair animal print pants with a chambray shirt or a simple tee! My go-to print? Leopard. What's yours?

## 7. Camo

_ Camo's been making its rounds in the fashion scene for years. A lot of people thought that it only worked for sporty or boyish looks but they're wrong! Camo pants can definitely be worn in a way that makes them feminine and stylish. For example, by pairing some camo skinnies (try [Macy's](http://www1.macys.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)) with a graphic sweatshirt like the ones from Forever 21_, you can achieve a polished look. Don't forget to add some accessories - but keep it simple because these pants are already enough of a statement!

Well, there you have it. These are just seven out of many awesome ways to wear printed pants this fall! Of the list, which look is your favorite? How do YOU like to wear the printed pants trend?

7 Street Style Ways to Wear Suspenders ...

With a Skater Skirt • Business Casual • With Baggy Jeans • With Trousers • Draped over One Shoulder • More ...

By Vanessa

There are so many fantastic street style ways to wear suspenders! If you're iffy about the trend, don't be. It's definitely making a fashion comeback and I've seen plenty of girls whose already-cool outfits have only been made cooler by them. Here are some street style ways to wear suspenders:

1. With a Skater Skirt

One of the easiest (and most popular) ways to wear suspenders is by wearing them with a black skater skirt! It's simple and always works because black looks good with anything! You can choose between a patterned top or a solid colored one to go with the skirt. This is just one of many street style ways to wear suspenders.

2. Business Casual

Take your office-ready outfit and spice it up a bit for a night out on the town. Whether you're headed for drinks after work or just like the business casual vibe, suspenders are a cute addition to your tailored outfit. I always love adding quirky and funky pieces to really feminine outfits!

3. With Baggy Jeans

Ever have those days where you feel like lounging around all day in oversized clothing? Yeah, me too. If you want to recreate this look, pair some baggy boyfriend jeans with a comfy oversized sweater. For the suspenders, if you want to keep things casual, pick a neutral color. If you want to be the standout, use a pop of color like any of these from kristenchef.com.

4. With Trousers

Adding suspenders to a sophisticated outfit is a fun and easy way to take your outfit to the next level. Since it's fall, don't forget the must-have fall accessories like a floppy hat and dark colors like navy blue!

5. Draped over One Shoulder

I would've never thought to wear it like this but I think it looks awesome! Instead of wearing them the traditional way, have the suspenders drape diagonally across your body. It's definitely a cool and fashion-forward way to wear suspenders and you'll look like such a trendsetter! I love the idea of pairing white trousers/suspenders with a pastel color. The warm colors remind me of warmer times and that's definitely what I'm needing with the current NYC weather!

6. Denim

Denim suspenders with denim skinnies? Yes, please! Skinny jeans are such a timeless item - they look great with pretty much anything and everything. Pair your pants with any color you'd like - I'd personally choose white because I think it has a great contrast! You can buy vintage denim suspenders from us.asos.com for a very cheap price!

7. Maxi Skirt

If you're not one for short skater skirts, try a maxi skirt! They're perfect for the fall season because of the breezy weather. You can find a skirt that has the suspenders already attached or you can personalize your outfit with a different set of suspenders; maybe a different color or pattern! I would keep the top half of the outfit sweet and simple with a collared button up or tee.

Well, there you have it. These are just seven out of many awesome street style ways to wear suspenders. The look is definitely making a comeback this season and I think, when worn properly, it makes for such a cute look! Of the list, which look is your favorite? What's YOUR favorite way to wear suspenders?

Frequently Asked Questions

Can women really pull off suspenders?
Absolutely! Suspenders aren't just for men. Women can totally rock them. It's all about confidence and pairing them with the right outfits.
What are some stylish ways to wear suspenders as a woman?
You can pair them with high-waisted trousers or a chic pencil skirt. Another cool option is to wear them over a blouse or a fitted t-shirt for a more casual vibe.
What should I wear with a suspender skirt?
A suspender skirt looks great with a tucked-in shirt or a cute crop top. Add some ankle boots or flats to complete the look. You can even layer with a jacket to add some edge.
Are suspenders suitable for office attire?
Definitely! Try wearing them with a crisp white shirt and tailored pants or a pencil skirt. Go for neutral colors to keep the look professional, but feel free to add a little flair with your accessories.
What kind of suspenders should I buy?
It depends on your style! Leather suspenders are great for a more vintage, classic look. Fabric ones can be fun and come in various colors and patterns. Adjustable ones are a good idea to ensure a perfect fit.

# 7 Street Style Ways to Wear the Colorblock Trend ...

There are so many fantastic street style ways to wear the colorblock trend. I'm a huge fan of this trend because it's really expressive and can be achieved in so many different ways! A lot of people are confused about what colorblocking even is... it just means wearing multiple solid colors in an outfit! Now do you realize how easy it is? Check out some of the cool ways to wear the colorblock trend and then let me know how YOU love to wear this style!

## 1. Snow White Inspired

![Snow White Inspired](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bx/iy/swetcgzq_586x880.jpg)

Who knew (besides Snow White) that yellow, red, and blue could look so good together? Don't shy away from pairing these colors together just because they're so famously known as the colors of a Disney princess's dress! I think they look absolutely fabulous together. You can colorblock this combination in many different ways. This is just one of many ways to wear the colorblock trend.

## 2. Matching Dress + Coat

![Matching Dress + Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g4/np/h4ij7pkc_600x895.jpg)

For double the colorblock look, wear a matching dress + coat combo! I actually really love the idea of layering the colors because it draws even more attention to them. Personally, I think any color paired with beige looks amazing. For shoes, nude pumps are perfect because they'll match any color that you decide to wear!

## 3. Dress

![Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gf/iu/ykm12mu0_600x901.jpg)

Colorblock dresses are a must-have item for every girl's closet. I definitely recommend stocking up on them because they're perfect for any occasion! You can find some at [Lord & Taylor](http://www.lordandtaylor.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com). So many colors look amazing together - what colors would you want to colorblock with?

## 4. Striped Sweater

![Striped Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/md/dl/rxcru1wr_590x885.jpg)

A super easy (and fun) way to wear this trend is with a colorful striped sweater. You can find some at [Neiman Marcus](http://www.neimanmarcus.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com). Since the sweater is already a statement piece, I'd keep the rest of the outfit simple. I'd pair it with some black/white shorts or pants. How many colors would you want on your sweater?

## 5. Crop Top + Midi

![Crop Top + Midi](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rw/v5/bogk6ktj_600x901.jpg)

The summertime is perfect for rocking some crop tops and midi skirts. You can easily wear the colorblock trend with an outfit like this! You can pair different neon colors for a major fashion statement, or, for a subtler tone, pair two different pastel colors. How about a baby blue top with a pastel pink skirt? The two colors look incredible together.

## 6. Trousers

![Trousers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g6/ec/g5n5x80a_600x900.jpg)

Colorblock trousers are great as a business casual look. Whether you're running errands or heading to meet your girlfriends for lunch, this look is sleek and sophisticated. You can find some colorblock trousers at [Neiman Marcus](http://www.neimanmarcus.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com). I'd pair them with a simple top. If you want to throw in even MORE color, add a colorful cross body bag to complete the look!

## 7. Mix & Match

![Mix & Match](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tv/y0/hubjb4j1_600x900.jpg)

One of the easiest ways to wear the colorblock trend is to mix and match different patterns. Remember, you can pull off anything if you walk around with your held up high. If you're not afraid of making a fashion statement then don't be afraid of wearing vibrant colors! As long as the colors and patterns coordinate in some way, you'll earn some major style points.

Well, there you have it. These are just seven out of many awesome street style ways to wear the colorblock trend. I love that there are so many different ways to achieve this look! Of the list, which was your favorite? Which look will you be recreating in the future?

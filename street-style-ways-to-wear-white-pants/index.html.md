# 9 Fab Street Style Ways to Wear White Pants ...

White clothing is certainly having a trending moment right now, and there are plenty of different ways to wear white pants this season. Work a crisp, sophisticated look in a pair of white pants. Dress them down for a fresh summer look or layer them up for a stylish winter outfit. If you’re unsure of how to make this trend work in your wardrobe, check out our pick of street style inspired ways to wear white pants below.

## 1. Neutral

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/07/1_neutral_426x639.jpg)

When it comes to ways to wear white pants, sticking to a neutral palette is a popular option. Lucinda Bell was snapped by Dvora for Vogue in this chic ensemble. She wears a Zara top, pants, and shoes. A slouchy Fendi bag and wayfarer sunglasses complete the look.

## 2. Prints

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/07/2_prints_426x639.jpg)

White pants are a great base for working a colourful look on your top half. Here, Beibei Wu was photographed wearing her white pants with a colourful Chloe top and Carven patterned jacket. The pants are from the H&M Conscious Collection. She finishes off her look with tan strappy sandals and a compact handbag. Switch the sandals for some pumps and this is the kind of outfit that could take you from work to evening drinks.

## 3. Palazzo

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/07/3_palazzo_500x750.jpg)

Candice Lake was snapped by Diego Zuka wearing this all-white ensemble. She teams white wide-legged palazzo pants with a white top, jacket, bag, and shoes. Brown sunglasses and a bright red lip are the only hints of colour in this entire outfit. Wide-legged pants like these are perfect for working a luxe look during summer, and would look equally as stylish with a slinky camisole and statement necklace.

## 4. Pastel

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/07/4_pastel_426x639.jpg)

White pants are great if you want to work a light, pastel look. Here, Dvora snapped Evelina Collin wearing white Monki pants. She paired her pants with an Acne lilac sweater, Karen Walker sunglasses, and a light cream coat. The bag and shoes were both from Zara. This is a great example of how to make your white pants transition from summer into winter.

## 5. Ripped

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/07/5_ripped_426x639.jpg)

Megha Kapoor was snapped by Candice Lake in this grungy looking outfit. She teams her ripped baggy white Bassike pants with a Michael Lo Sordo top and Prada shoes. The metallic clutch and reflective sunglasses complete her look.

## 6. Slouchy

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/07/6_slouchy_600x900.jpg)

Alexia was shot for Caroline’s Mode wearing these slouchy white pants. They have quite a relaxed look to them, thanks to their slouchy cut and drawstring waist. She teams her white pants with a sheer leopard print top and neutral booties for a put-together daytime look.

## 7. Jumpsuit

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/07/7_jumpsuit_600x900.jpg)

OK, so technically this is a jumpsuit, but it’s still a stylish way to work a white outfit. This young lady was snapped by Mr. Newman for Harper’s Bazaar. She teams her relaxed white jumpsuit with grey pumps, a studded clutch, and assorted bangles. If you don’t own a white jumpsuit (and I can’t say that many of us would), then you could easily recreate this look by tucking a white top into a pair of white pants.

## 8. Jeans

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/07/8_jeans_426x639.jpg)

White jeans are a casual way to wear this look. Here, Camilla Pryce was snapped for Vogue in a colourful looking outfit. She teams her white jeans with an electric blue H&M coat, coral tee, leopard print shoes, and a tan tote. This look is fresh and fun, and is ideal for running around town.

## 9. Colour

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/07/9_colour_600x900.jpg)

Blogger Jessica Stein pairs her white pants with citrus colours. She teams her wide-legged pants with a boxy yellow top and an orange clutch. It’s a refreshing look that’s well suited for summertime get-togethers and afternoon cocktails.

These are just a few different ways that we’ve seen white pants being styled. How would you style white pants? Which of these street style looks do you like best?

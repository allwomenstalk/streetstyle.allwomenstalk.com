# 7 Street Style Looks from Music Festivals That Are Perfect to Recreate ...

There are so many adorable street style looks from music festivals that are perfect to recreate! These are just a few simple looks that can be dressed up or down - whichever you want! Music festivals are just as much about fashion as they are about music! Check out some of these awesome street style looks from music festivals that you can recreate.

## 1. Crop Top + Mini

![Crop Top + Mini](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u1/vl/bn58jx1r_432x648.jpg)

A class music festival outfit is a crop top + mini skirt. You can either mix and match patterns/colors, or go for the matching set. Personally, I love mixing and matching! I love this outfit and the simplicity of it. To recreate this look, pair a white crop top with a printed high-waisted skirt. Converse, Vans, or even combat boots would look great with this outfit! Don't forget your cross body bag so you have the perfect place to store your phone. This look is just one of the many street style looks from music festivals you should recreate.

## 2. Floral

![Floral](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eb/rh/dsi5vz20_600x900.jpg)

My go-to pattern is always floral. Always. This year, try out a floral dress and pair it with a fun floral headband. Nothing says summer music festival like having daises on your dress! It's fun and youthful and will keep you looking fab while you listen to your favorite bands.

## 3. Casual

![Casual](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fm/ty/zmpes5o1_500x750.jpg)

Julianne Hough always look fabulous in a comfy, casual outfit. To recreate this look, pair a white button up shirt with some distressed denim cutoffs. Add your favorite cross body bag, a hat, and your boots! Don't forget your sunglasses, you'll be needing them! With this outfit, you'll be comfortable and stylish all at the same time. Kudos, Julianne!

## 4. Rebel Chic

![Rebel Chic](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/il/so/x5os1bvn_600x856.jpg)

No one does rebel chic better than Pretty Little Liars' Ashley Benson. If you want to go for this look, it's easy! Rock some ripped black skinnies, classic Converse, your favorite graphic tee, and a snapback! It'll make you look like a total badass, and that's always a good thing.

## 5. Crop Top + High Waisted Shorts

![Crop Top + High Waisted Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bl/vz/s764m72w_600x900.jpg)

The go-to music festival outfit is usually a crop top and high waisted shorts! It's a simple outfit but can easily be dressed up to add some major style points. Add a floppy hat, round sunglasses, cross body bag, and combat boots! They'll add some edge to the outfit and take it to the next level! I'm loving the lace detail on this crop top!

## 6. Flowy Dress

![Flowy Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ma/wd/d8w5mbra_420x630.jpg)

You can never go wrong with a long flowy dress! If you go with white, however, be extra careful about sitting on the ground! It might be tough but hey, you'll look great. Pair the dress with some colorful accessories. It'll add a fun and youthful vibe to the outfit!

## 7. Fringes

![Fringes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bw/jv/y5fsrk49_500x750.jpg)

I'm obsessed with this look! It's such a simple outfit but it's so rocker-chic! It's the perfect way to look effortlessly cool at whatever music festival you're heading to. Pair a black crop top with a fringed skirt. Don't forget your combat boots and leather jacket! Black accessories and sun glasses will help take the outfit to the next level.

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of many different looks that are perfect to recreate for any music festival. What do you like to wear to them? Of the list, which look is your favorite?

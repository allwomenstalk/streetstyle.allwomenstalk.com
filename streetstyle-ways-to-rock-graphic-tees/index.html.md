# 7 Streetstyle Ways to Rock Graphic Tees ...

There are so many great ways to wear graphic tees! Nowadays, it's all about having smart slogans, your favorite band's name, some clever quote, or a sweet retro graphic, written across your shirt. I'll admit, graphic tees are definitely the go-to item in my closet! There are actually so many great ways to dress them up. Check out some of my favorite style ideas!

## 1. Flared Pants

![Flared Pants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ew/hz/ssrmdkv8_554x747.jpg)

I'm not entirely sure I'd be able to pull these off, but no one does flared pants better than Lily Collins. It's definitely an awesome hipster-chic way to rock graphic tees! So, if you've got some hipster in you, try this look out! Rock some printed flared pants with a tee that has your favorite band's logo. You can't go wrong with that! This is just one of the cool ways to style your graphic tees.

## 2. Pencil Skirt

![Pencil Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gk/gb/ospnwsdy_400x600.jpg)

Who knew graphic tees + a pencil skirt looked so good together?! This is definitely a look worth recreating this spring/summer. I love the fact that this outfit isn't very "match-matchy". The top and bottom half are two totally different patterns but still, they look great together. It's definitely giving out an "effortlessly cool" vibe!

## 3. Blazer

![Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t3/ch/qf4fsewp_600x900.jpg)

An easy way to dress up your graphic tee is to throw a blazer over it! It just gives any outfit a more "sophisticated" feel to it. I love that this outfit looks great with sneakers and would look just as great with some black pumps. If you want, you could even swap the white blazer for a more colorful one! A red one would look great over this graphic tee!

## 4. All-black Leather

![All-black Leather](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c4/p3/kxnijrob_451x750.jpg)

If you're going a for a "badass, tough girl" look, leather is the way to go. Rock some leather leggings, black pumps, and throw a black graphic tee over it. I'm especially loving this tee of a dog growling! It definitely adds to the whole "tough girl" feel. What kind of graphic tee would you pair with the bottom half of this outfit?

## 5. Colored Suit

![Colored Suit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hv/sm/z4q59vfk_600x900.jpg)

This is another look that I think is awesome but wouldn't really be able to pull off. Colored suits are definitely going to be big this spring/summer though! So, if you want something that'll make you look professional AND fierce, try this look out. I like this graphic tee but of course, you can swap it out for any other that you'd like better!

## 6. Denim Shorts

![Denim Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/po/vg/ml2ep2zn_600x836.jpg)

Denim shorts are definitely the "usual" for pairing with graphic tees. I'm loving this all-white ensemble! White denim looks great in the summer when your skin is freshly sun-kissed! It's also great because it'll match any colored tee.

## 7. Boyfriend Jeans

![Boyfriend Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yc/az/ogu5jemn_600x900.jpg)

One of the easiest ways to rock graphic tees is to pair them with your boyfriend jeans! Like graphic tees, boyfriend jeans can be dressed up or dressed down. I love that it's dressed up in this look! I'm loving the black heels, leather jacket, and pop of color in that red clutch. Definitely a look worth recreating this spring.

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of many awesome ways to rock graphic tees. They're insanely popular at the moment especially with all the smart slogans people come up with! Of the list, which is your favorite look?

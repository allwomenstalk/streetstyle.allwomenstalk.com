# 7 Streetstyle Ways to Wear a Winter Hat ...

You can't step out of your house and into the cold without covering your ears. That's why you need to buy the perfect hat. Don't worry, because they can actually look fashionable. According to [Fashion Magazine,](https://fashionmagazine.com/style/how-to-wear-a-winter-hat/8/) here are a few ways to wear a winter hat:

## 1. Grey Beanie

![Grey Beanie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n0/o2/zpnpsodb56ab7f7b9186f489169992_600x927.jpg)

You can never go wrong with an artsy beanie.

## 2. Poof on Top

![Poof on Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c2/kn/bqcr0oe856ab7f7c196d8393758981_600x900.jpg)

If you wear a hat with plenty of designs on it, then you'll look fashionable without having to do any extra work.

## 3. Bear Ears

![Bear Ears](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qu/at/at7mui5u56ab7f7c08946505170476_600x840.jpg)

You can't hibernate, but you can get fashion inspiration from an animal who does!

## 4. White Beanie

![White Beanie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ez/i9/ggdfkc8556ab7f7c484a8336437174_600x900.jpg)

Try wearing a hat that matches the snow!

## 5. Mismatched

![Mismatched](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dg/jt/xilwf76956ab7f7c73923331540604_600x900.jpg)

You don't have to wear a hat that matches the color of your outfit, you know.

## 6. With Glasses

![With Glasses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qo/je/gc1v502156ab7f7c90796956396410_600x906.jpg)

Why stop at one accessory? Wear glasses with your hat!

## 7. Plain Black

![Plain Black](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/do/u2/hl3eo5fj56ab7f7d35e69235370282_600x902.jpg)

Black matches every outfit, so you can't go wrong when buying a hat like this.

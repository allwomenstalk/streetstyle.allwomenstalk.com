# 7 Streetstyle Ways to Wear Converse and Rock It ...

There are so many awesome ways to wear converse sneakers! With spring/summer coming up, wardrobes are changing! With these styling ideas, converse will become your go-to item. They look good with just about everything and that's why the converse "trend" will never go away! Check out some ways to wear converse and let me know which look you'll be rocking next!

## 1. With Leather

![With Leather](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dr/ua/u0/d9mzn9oq_600x896.jpg)

Personally, I think leather gives a "bad-ass" feel to any outfit. That's why I love when it's paired with converse! It's the perfect balance between rock-chic and casual. This look is great for summer and running errands around the city! For this look, you can rock denim shorts, a grey tee, and then pair it with your leather jacket and black converse. The studs on the jacket are an awesome touch! The oversized bag and sun glasses add to the outfit too! This is just one of the awesome ways to wear converse.

## 2. Maxi Dress

![Maxi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b4/ll/vr/wpz4kpdq_600x900.jpg)

I love this look! Maxi dresses are perfect for spring/summer. I love the leather jacket over the dress and the leather cross body bag! The white converse look great with the neutral colored dress; black would also work! If you want, swap the maxi dress for a maxi skirt and wear a cropped top under the leather jacket!

## 3. Tights and Shorts

![Tights and Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/es/uu/qb/p5uretbh_496x750.jpg)

With the weather getting warmer, shorts are starting to make their appearance on the streets! If you think your shorts are TOO short, wear some tights underneath them! The converse are a really cool touch to this outfit.

## 4. Casual

![Casual](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wd/ni/r3/u8bwxet4_372x604.jpg)

Overall, converse give any outfit a "casual" feel. If you're just taking a stroll in the park or just hanging out with friends, pair your converse with some jeans and a sweater! In this look, the jeans are baggy and the sweatshirt is fitted. However, you can wear it the opposite way! Oversized sweater and skinny jeans. That's how I love to wear it!

## 5. Pop of Color

![Pop of Color](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ri/xr/zf/p7w37pgl_600x920.jpg)

Some people forget that converse aren't JUST black and white! They come in a huge variety of colors. My go-to color is red because it never clashes with anything! If you're wearing an outfit made up of neutral colors, try adding colorful converse! It'll spice up your outfit and you'll look super cool/cute!

## 6. Mini Dress

![Mini Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yw/l8/qe/cjkvoj0f_600x855.jpg)

I've seen so many girls rock this look in New York City! The mini dress/shirt-dress is a perfect look for the hot weather. I love that this dress, that would usually be paired with some heels, looks just as great paired with some white converse! By keeping your shoes simple, you can play around with fun patterns on your dress! The sunglasses and black cross body bag help keep the outfit casual and trendy.

## 7. All-black Ensemble

![All-black Ensemble](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/0e/u1/wl/gj4aua2v_600x825.jpg)

I'm a huge fan of all-black ensembles! Maybe not on a blazing hot day though. I think the all-black looks are very sleek and sophisticated. I love this outfit because even though it's simple, it makes her look like a fashionista. The up 'do adds to the whole "effortlessly cool" look that's going on.

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of many different ways you can style your converse sneakers! How do you love to wear them out? Which look was your favorite?

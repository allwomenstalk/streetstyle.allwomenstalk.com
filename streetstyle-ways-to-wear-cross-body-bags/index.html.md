# 7 Streetstyle Ways to Wear Cross Body Bags ...

There are tons of awesome ways to wear cross body bags! With spring/summer coming up, it is sure to become your new go-to item! I love them because they're such a great complement to any outfit. With these ways to wear cross body bags, you'll always look fashionable!

## 1. Denim on Denim

![Denim on Denim](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yt/h0/j5/0l4dtfs4_550x825.jpg)

Some people aren't a fan of it, but I love denim on denim. It's just so much that it actually works. Does that make sense? This look is great! I love the blue aviators and the blue leather cross body bag. If you don't want to seem too "matchy-matchy", swap the blue bag for another color! It'll still look just as awesome. This is just one of the fun ways to wear cross body bags!

## 2. Fun Print

![Fun Print](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lm/vl/hc/smde9845_600x865.jpg)

You never go wrong with a bold print. If your outfit is really simple and made up of neutral colors, add a printed bag! It'll take your outfit to the next level! I really love everything about this look. The jeans, boots, tee, and blazer all look great together. What I love the most though is the cheetah-print cross body bag!

## 3. Mini Cross Body

![Mini Cross Body](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k9/cc/g2/d5tnfd5t_600x900.jpg)

Usually, cross body bags are small. However, there are ones that can be considered TINY! If you're just planning on throwing in your phone and lip gloss, this type of bag would be great for you. You can find solid colors or fun prints like this floral one! Mini cross body bags will complement your outfit without taking attention away from it! Perfect.

## 4. Pop of Neon

![Pop of Neon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vb/qa/zk/rsg6putk_600x855.jpg)

Neon is always a good idea. If you're wearing neutral colors, or even an all-white ensemble, a neon bag is the perfect splash of color! I particularly love this green one with the studded details! Don't be afraid to mix patterns and colors. You can rock anything with confidence!

## 5. Pastel Colors

![Pastel Colors](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j0/r5/av/ioz90gwb_471x700.jpg)

A big trend for this spring is pastels! They're soft, sweet colors that look good on anyone! I love this pastel blue bag, paired with a flowing white dress. It looks great together! You can also opt for pastel shades of pink, purple, etc. What's your favorite pastel color?

## 6. Oversized

![Oversized](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vx/wu/ef/nqf4tcff_600x900.jpg)

I love oversized fashion! Whether it be bags, sunglasses, or sweaters, it's always a cute look. I love this oversized cross body bag paired with a maxi skirt and sheer top. The color is everything! You could also swap the maxi skirt/top for a more casual look like jeans and a graphic tee!

## 7. Fringes

![Fringes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yv/u8/r2/gk5cldf7_592x888.jpg)

For those who think fringes are "out", you're wrong. They're still totally in! I love the fringe detail on this beige cross body! It definitely adds a fun, youthful vibe to the look. The color is great because you can pair it with any colored dress or patterns! I'm definitely on the look out for one of these bags!

Well, there you have it! This is just seven out of many awesome ways to wear cross body bags. I have a feeling cross body bags will be all the rage this spring/summer season! Stock up on all these different styles so you'll always look great! How do you love to wear your cross body bag? Share tips in the comments section! Of the list, which look is your favorite?

# Style 101: Heres Your Fall Shopping Checklist!

Shopping for your 2014 fall wardrobe is about to get a whole lot easier. There are certain pieces you just NEED to have and this checklist will help you make sure you have everything! Check out some of the must-haves for your 2014 fall wardrobe.

## 1. Oxford Shirt

![white,black,clothing,footwear,high heeled footwear,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f7/wr/k6ve9_white_black_clothing_footwear_high-heeled-footwear_600x900.jpg)

A good oxford shirt is essential to every fall wardrobe. There are endless outfit possibilities that you can create with this one item! You can pair it with some leather leggings, a black skater skirt, skinny jeans, etc. It's a timeless look that will 'class' up any of your outfits. Try [Tommy Hilfiger](http://usa.tommy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) for your oxford shirt needs! This is just one of many items you need for your 2014 fall wardrobe.

## 2. Flannel

![clothing,plaid,pattern,design,tartan,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q3/rj/r36es_clothing_plaid_pattern_design_tartan_600x855.jpg)

Flannels just scream out fall. It's perfect for the traditional pumpkin picking dates and a cool addition to any outfit. Whether you're actually wearing the flannel or have it tied around your waist, it should definitely be a part of your fall wardrobe. You can head to [Forever 21](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) for all your flannel needs. Everything's super cute and very affordable!

## 3. Oversized Sweater

![clothing,brown,outerwear,footwear,pattern,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zo/w9/y1516_clothing_brown_outerwear_footwear_pattern_370x530.jpg)

Honestly, does anything a beat an oversized sweater? It gives you the two things every girl strives for: comfort and style. You get the best of both worlds! Try [sheinside.com](https://goo.gl/rVtgMH) to find the oversized sweater that's best for you!

## 4. Turtleneck

![clothing,yellow,lady,pattern,fashion,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kw/h3/dev0m_clothing_yellow_lady_pattern_fashion_580x869.jpg)

Who said turtlenecks aren't fashionable anymore? They're perfect for the fall! You can find some cute ones at [American Apparel](https://goo.gl/7YRGyF) and they come in every color!

## 5. Midi Skirt

![clothing,dress,yellow,spring,fashion,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jv/o4/ifd6c_clothing_dress_yellow_spring_fashion_400x600.jpg)

Midi skirts are the perfect balance between cutesy and conventional. They're great for the fall because of the breezy weather! Try [Modcloth](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) for your midi skirt needs and don't forget to pair it with a patterned top!

## 6. Shift Dress

![clothing,black,sleeve,dress,footwear,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/il/tf/dle37_clothing_black_sleeve_dress_footwear_500x713.jpg)

Shift dresses are great because they're flattering for any body type. Add a fun accessory like a chunky necklace or a belt to make the dress stand out! Try [Nordstrom](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) for some cool patterned dresses.

## 7. Wide Leg Trousers

![white,clothing,dress,footwear,spring,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oz/t7/l1y3k_white_clothing_dress_footwear_spring_500x710.jpg)

Wide leg trousers are for those who want to look [business chic](https://streetstyle.allwomenstalk.com/street-style-ways-to-look-business-chic/)! The flared pant keeps you feeling comfortable all day. Find your favorite from the collection at [H&M](http://www.hm.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

## 8. Belted Jacket

![white,clothing,footwear,winter,coat,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/an/hl/d32bx_white_clothing_footwear_winter_coat_585x878.jpg)

Since summer's over, it's time to start stocking up on some jackets! A belted jacket like this one from [Bloomingdale's](http://www1.bloomingdales.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) will keep you looking like a fashionista.

## 9. Puffer Vest

![clothing,photography,girl,beauty,portrait photography,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oz/dq/s0jhq_clothing_photography_girl_beauty_portrait-photography_600x891.jpg)

Fall's just started and I've already seen this trend everywhere. Puffer vests are a must-have! I've become obsessed with the Excursion vest from [J.Crew](https://goo.gl/yth7Wd). I definitely need to buy it soon!

## 10. Trench Coat

![clothing,road,coat,footwear,outerwear,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w0/ql/jxxk2_clothing_road_coat_footwear_outerwear_540x810.jpg)

A good trench coat will leave you feeling like you're ready to take over the world. Channel your inner Blair Waldorf with a jacket from [Macy's](http://www1.macys.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

## 11. Comfy Loafers

![footwear,clothing,shoe,high heeled footwear,jeans,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k1/x4/toumi_footwear_clothing_shoe_high-heeled-footwear_jeans_500x658.jpg)

Loafers are definitely a fall essential. I'm in love with the ones from [Henri Bendel](https://www.henribendel.com/accessories/footwear/loafers/), they're super stylish and comfortable! You can wear them with skinny jeans and pair it with a cozy sweater.

## 12. Floppy Hat

![clothing,pattern,footwear,outerwear,fashion,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fi/ce/rn1cw_clothing_pattern_footwear_outerwear_fashion_600x891.jpg)

What's more fall-like than a floppy hat? You can wear it with anything and look stylish!

## 13. Structured Purse

![bag,handbag,red,leather,fashion accessory,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e0/kl/b9fzo_bag_handbag_red_leather_fashion-accessory_600x900.jpg)

The new purse trend? Structured purses! You can find cute and affordable options at [Forever 21](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) that'll look great with any outfit.

## 14. Tights

![clothing,footwear,coat,winter,fur,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qu/tk/p6axg_clothing_footwear_coat_winter_fur_415x594.jpg)

As cute as sheer tights are, I think opaque tights are better for the fall. You can buy some from [H&M](http://www.hm.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)!

## 15. Infinity Scarf

![clothing,brown,fashion accessory,fur clothing,fur,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fv/vw/cn9bq_clothing_brown_fashion-accessory_fur-clothing_fur_500x701.jpg)

No fall look is complete without an infinity scarf. I practically live in these during the fall.

How many of these items do you already own?

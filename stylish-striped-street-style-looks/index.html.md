# 10 Stylish Striped Street Style Looks You May Want to Try ...

Striped street style looks seem to be everywhere at the moment. From the classic Breton to bold and bright coloured stripes, fashionistas the world over are loving all things striped. Stripes are a classic pattern and can be interpreted in so many different ways. Keep things classic with a striped top and skinny jeans or go a bit crazy and mix up your prints. Take a look at these stylish striped street style looks.

## 1. Cool and Casual Striped Skirt

![Cool and Casual Striped Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/06/1_cool-and-casual-striped-skirt_518x944.jpg)

Striped street style looks can come in all different forms. Here, Martha Hunt is snapped by Phil Oh for Vogue wearing a bold striped skirt. She toughens up the prim and proper full skirt by teaming it with a cropped tank top and black ankle boots. If you’re not too keen on crop tops you can always recreate this outfit by wearing a tank top tucked into the skirt instead.

## 2. Bold Striped T-Shirt

![Bold Striped T-Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/06/2_bold-striped-t-shirt_592x888.jpg)

Stripes and denim are a classic combination. Karen Overton was photographed by Suzanne Middlemass wearing a bold striped top, denim shorts, Chanel tote, and Converse hi-top trainers. The bright pink sunglasses add a fun element to the entire outfit.

## 3. Striped Maxi Dress

![Striped Maxi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/06/3_striped-maxi-dress_600x900.jpg)

A flowing maxi dress is a summer staple. Choose one in a striped pattern to stay on top of trends this season. Jourdan Dunn was captured by Vanessa Jackman teaming a black and white striped maxi dress with a floppy hat and chic satchel bag. This is the kind of outfit that can take you straight from the beach to the city streets.

## 4. Slouchy Striped Skirt

![Slouchy Striped Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/06/4_slouchy-striped-skirt_600x900.jpg)

Mirte Maas was shot by Mr. Newton looking cool and casual in a drapey striped skirt. The red and cream striped skirt is the focus point of the outfit, and she teams it with a simple navy long-sleeved top and brown ankle boots. While the navy/red/cream colour combination is a classic, this is much more of a dressed-down and modern take on it.

## 5. Striped Coat

![Striped Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/06/5_striped-coat_480x720.jpg)

Designer Maayan Zilberman was spotted on the Tory Burch blog working a sportswear inspired look in a striped coat. She topped off a simple combination of a shirt and shorts with a black and white Tory Burch striped coat. Printed sneakers and a bright red lip added some playfulness to the entire look.

## 6. Ladylike Striped Dress

![Ladylike Striped Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/06/6_ladylike-striped-dress_599x898.jpg)

Blogger Blair Eadie from Atlantic-Pacific works a ladylike look in a striped dress and white blazer from Zara. She stays in the blue and white theme by accessorising with a compact blue bag, oversized white sunglasses, and blue bow heels.

## 7. Spots and Stripes

![Spots and Stripes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/06/7_spots-and-stripes_592x888.jpg)

Chiara Marchionni was snapped by Suzanne Middlemass for Glamour Magazine wearing this playful outfit. She mixed her prints by teaming a Cos striped t-shirt with Uniqlo spotted pants. She tied the look together with a black vintage jacket, neutral clutch, and strappy sandals. If you’re keen to mix and match your prints, take a leaf out of Chiara’s book and stick to similar colours to keep the look streamlined.

## 8. Striped Biker Jacket

![Striped Biker Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/06/8_striped-biker-jacket_600x900.jpg)

Here, Sara from Collage Vintage works a fun street-smart look. She teams a striped Zara jacket with a Mickey Mouse tee (which you unfortunately can’t see here), skinny jeans, and black Converse trainers. A bright pink American Apparel beanie adds a pop of colour to the entire outfit.

## 9. Striped Trousers

![Striped Trousers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/06/9_striped-trousers_550x825.jpg)

Striped trousers are having a trending moment. Christine from Fash-n-Chips works a stylish look in wide-legged Zara Trousers and a chunky knit sweater. Make this work in the summertime by ditching the chunky knit for a slouchy tee or singlet instead.

## 10. Stripes and Leather

![Stripes and Leather](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/06/10_stripes-and-leather_600x906.jpg)

Add some edge to a monochrome look by incorporating a bit of leather into your outfit. Marina Larroude was spotted by Phil Oh wearing this black and white top and an asymmetrical leather skirt. Strappy heels and a compact white clutch completed her look.

These are just a few striped street style looks that have caught our attention recently. How do you wear your stripes? Which of these striped street style looks do you like best?

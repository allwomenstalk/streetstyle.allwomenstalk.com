# 7 Summery and Fabulous Street Style Ways to Wear Leather ...

Stay on top of current trends and find different ways to wear leather in the summer. Sure, you might be more used to seeing leather jackets and trousers worn during winter, but there are lots of ways you can make your leather work in the summer too. Stick to leather pieces that are relaxed and breezy, so you avoid any sweaty hot-weather leather situations. Take inspiration from these street style snaps and look at these ways to wear leather for summer.

## 1. Black Leather Pinafore

![Black Leather Pinafore](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/07/1_black-leather-pinafore_600x903.jpg)

When it comes to different ways to wear leather this summer, stay cool in a leather or leather-look pinafore. This lovely lady was snapped by Rachel Yabsley for Australian high street store, Sportsgirl. She works a minimalist urban look, rocking a relaxed leather pinafore, muscle tee, and flat ankle boots. Rounded sunglasses and a slouchy handbag complete this black and white daytime look.

## 2. Leather Mini Skirt

![Leather Mini Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/07/2_leather-mini-skirt_600x899.jpg)

Play up a simple summer outfit with kooky accessories. Adam Katz Sinding photographed this lovely lady accessorising her basic outfit of a black leather mini skirt and white tank top with a colourful visor, clear plastic handbag, and two-tone platform sandals.

## 3. Tan Leather Skirt

![Tan Leather Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/07/3_tan-leather-skirt_600x900.jpg)

Steer away from black leather and try a light tan leather for summer. We spotted this look over on Refinery 29 and think it could easily make a chic summer outfit. Instead of a furry sweater, opt for a slouchy tee or tank top. Oversized sunglasses, a small side bag, and strappy sandals complete the look.

## 4. Black Leather Shorts

![Black Leather Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/07/4_black-leather-shorts_600x954.jpg)

Leather shorts are a seriously on-trend option for summer dressing. They can look much more dressed-up than your usual denim shorts and are versatile enough to be worn both during the day and night. We spotted this young lady featured on the Stylesight blog working a simply stylish look in leather shorts and a white t-shirt. Black strappy sandals, a black side bag, and ribbed beanie topped off this black and white look.

## 5. Leather Harness Vest

![Leather Harness Vest](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/07/5_leather-harness-vest_432x648.jpg)

Toughen up your usual chiffon maxi skirt with a leather top. Aleksandra was spotted by Elle Magazine wearing a flowing cream maxi, white singlet, and black leather harness vest. The vest definitely adds a dark edge to an otherwise feminine look. You could also try leather shell tops and cropped designs to wear with [high waisted skirts](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-reasons-to-love-high-waisted-skirts/) this summer.

## 6. Leather T-Shirt

![Leather T-Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/07/6_leather-t-shirt_540x813.jpg)

Swap your normal cotton t-shirt for a leather version this summer. Leather t-shirts are great for working a dressed-up daytime look. Black leather t-shirts are probably the most versatile, but try white or coloured leather for a fresher approach. Blogger ThankFifi paired her cobalt leather tee with printed shorts and studded sandals for a chic summertime look.

## 7. Coloured Leather Dress

![Coloured Leather Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/streetstyle/2013/07/7_coloured-leather-dress_600x900.jpg)

Summer is a great time to experiment with colour. Instead of your usual black leather, try experimenting with different coloured leather clothing. Here, Katie Mossman was snapped by Jason Jean for Citizen Couture wearing a statement-making red shift dress. She keeps the rest of her accessories to a minimum with strappy black sandals, a chain strap side bag, and wayfarer style sunglasses.

Take a cue from these ladies and try rocking leather clothing this summer. If real leather isn’t your thing then you can always try the faux leather variety. Which of these leather street style looks do you like best?

# Transition Your Summer Wardrobe to a Fall One ...

As the seasons change, your wardrobe should too. It's easy to transition from summer looks to fall ones with these simple fashion tips!

## 1. Swap Your Scandals for Combat Boots

![Swap Your Scandals for Combat Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/st/vh/qsmwht9i_600x922.jpg)

Combat boots are comfy and cute. They go with almost anything and leave you feeling like you're ready to tackle the day.

## 2. Swap Your Headbands for Floppy Hats

![Swap Your Headbands for Floppy Hats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p0/wq/wgy5ukor_500x749.jpg)

A floppy hat is a great way to accessorize your fall outfit without looking like you're trying too hard. You can coordinate the color with your outfit or a make a fashion statement with a fun and bold color!

## 3. Swap Your Crop Tops for Cardigans

![Swap Your Crop Tops for Cardigans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f7/qi/m8bgak92_600x927.jpg)

Cardigans are an easy way to stay cozy in the breezy weather. You can choose ones from [Forever 21](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) because they're cute AND affordable!

## 4. Swap Your Lip Gloss for Dark Lips

![Swap Your Lip Gloss for Dark Lips](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q8/wk/onpgv9bd_400x600.jpg)

Summer is all about glossy lips but now it's time to switch things up. For the fall, rock some dark lips! Red lips are always classic but my personal favorite is a dark edgy color like burgundy.

## 5. Add Knee High Socks

![Add Knee High Socks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qr/g1/vnuqavac_457x750.jpg)

Knee high socks keep you warm and earn you some major style points. My favorites are the colorful ones from [Nordstrom](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

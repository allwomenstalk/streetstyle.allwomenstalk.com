# Trend Alert: Denim on Denim ...

Denim-on-denim used to be a big fashion "don't," but with big stars showing us how it's done, the trend is back and better than ever. Check out some of the stars that have been spotted wearing the trend and how they spruced it up with some feminine touches!

## 1. Jessica Alba

![Jessica Alba](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oh/kt/w6wy5uah_600x935.jpg)

Jessica stepped out in NYC in head-to-toe light-wash denim! The white pumps instantly turns this casual outfit into a casual-chic one!

## 2. Selena Gomez

![Selena Gomez](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xv/ez/tmsm6qw1_400x600.jpg)

Selena paired some dark-wash high-wasited jeans with a light-wash oversized denim jacket. I love that she cut the jacket to give it a better fit!

## 3. Kate Walsh

![Kate Walsh](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y6/gp/ep1p7245_600x780.jpg)

Kate Walsh rocked loose-fitting boyfriend jeans, matched with a similarly toned chambray long sleeved shirt! The shoes and bag add an instant feminine touch to the look.

## 4. Olivia Munn

![Olivia Munn](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sz/ov/p1wn11tw_471x750.jpg)

Keeping the boyfriend jean trend going, Olivia wore some distressed ones paired with a light-wash chambray shirt! I like that she kept the accessories to a minimum because the denim-on-denim look is already enough of a fashion statement.

## 5. Kristin Cavallari

![Kristin Cavallari](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g8/vx/e5xhiz6g_600x797.jpg)

Kristen paired some light-wash skinny jeans with a light-wash chambray long sleeved shirt and added some two-toned pumps to complete the look!

## 6. Rihanna

![Rihanna](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w3/m5/p9cu6hwd_482x724.jpg)

Rihanna mastered the denim on denim look by pairing a light-wash chambray shirt with a distressed denim pencil skirt!

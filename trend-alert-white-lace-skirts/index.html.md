# Trend Alert: White Lace Skirts!

In the past, wearing white after Labor Day was a MAJOR no-no but rules were made to be broken, right ladies? If you can look this good in white, go for it - no matter what time of the year it is! Check out some of the celebrities that have been wearing the latest style trend, white lace skirts!

## 1. Shay Mitchell

![Shay Mitchell](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ou/u0/ya6frfm3_400x610.jpg)

Shay stunned with a lace pencil skirt and matching bustier at this year's Teen Choice Awards!

## 2. Kim Kardashian

![Kim Kardashian](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q4/rh/xcisemyi_600x850.jpg)

While celebrating her birthday in Vegas, Kim rocked the same white lace trend, pairing a pencil skirt with a matching bustier.

## 3. Ariana Grande

![Ariana Grande](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a9/gs/vjkr8doo_600x969.jpg)

Ariana wore the white lace trend at the Stand Up to Cancer event earlier this month where she honored her late grandfather with a moving performance of her song, "My Everything."

## 4. Jaime Chung

![Jaime Chung](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kl/ut/mvdvnu04_585x857.jpg)

Jaime made the trend a bit more casual by pairing it with a silk floral crop top. Of course, you can make the look more your own by swapping the crop top for a fun t-shirt!

## 5. Taissa Farmiga

![Taissa Farmiga](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/td/hs/xw988y9q_400x603.jpg)

At this year's Emmy awards, American Horror Story's Taissa Farmiga's look was embellished with white lace detailing layered on top of her dress.

Will you be wearing this trend anytime soon?

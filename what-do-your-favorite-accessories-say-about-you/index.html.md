# What do Your Favorite Accessories Say about You?

Accessories can make or break an entire outfit. Everyone has that one thing they just can't be seen without and that says a lot about the kind of person they are! Check out what your favorite accessory says about you:

## 1. Oversized Bag

![Oversized Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/as/iq/wh23h60z_600x900.jpg)

An oversized bag means you're a gal who's always on the go. That means you have to be prepared for anything at anytime. So, you practically carry your entire bedroom in your purse! Your friends may give you grief about your tendency to overpack but hey, you NEVER know when something will come in handy! This proves that you're a critical and practical thinker.

## 2. Bracelets & Bangles

![Bracelets & Bangles](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t6/mq/jgavfyez_400x600.jpg)

If you love accessorizing with bracelets, it's because you're a chatterbox who loves to show them off whilst talking. Yup, you're one of those people who can't tell a story without reenacting it all with hand gestures. LOL, hey, at least your hands look good!

## 3. Statement Earrings

![Statement Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tb/pz/v1qwj4eh_600x900.jpg)

When it comes to fashion, you have no fear of expressing your personal style. You love making a statement with attention-grabbing earrings, whether they're classy and elegant or cool and quirky. You're also a fan of knowing that statement earrings draw attention to that lovely face of yours!

## 4. Headphones

![Headphones](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pr/pn/ub9n1etv_570x731.jpg)

Headphones are my favorite accessory too... partly because I wish my life was set to a soundtrack. If this is true for you, you don't just like music; you NEED it to get through your long days. You love to surround yourself with art, books, and films that are inspiring. You're a creative powerhouse!

## 5. Quirky Phone Case

![Quirky Phone Case](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sl/bz/e54cgx5f_600x824.jpg)

You're pretty much addicted to phone cases and have a whole stack of them in your room! This says that you’re an eccentric. You march to the beat of your own drum and love being unique and different. You don't really care what others think about the things you buy because they make you happy!

What's your favorite accessory that you just can't live without?
